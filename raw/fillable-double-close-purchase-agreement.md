# Fillable Double-Close Purchase Agreement

**Description:** Fillable purchase and sale agreement for wholesale/double-close transactions.
**Buyer:** Webber Investment Homes LLC and/or assigns

## Key Provisions

**Parties:**
- Seller (property owner)
- Buyer: Webber Investment Homes LLC and/or assigns

**Purchase Terms:**
- Sales Price (Cash Portion + Financing options)
- Options: Third Party Financing, Loan Assumption, Seller Financing, Subject to Existing Loan

**Title Policy:** Owner and Loan title policies; closing costs — party split as agreed

**Survey:** Existing survey provided by Seller; new survey at specified party's expense

**Earnest Money:** Specified amount delivered to escrow agent within specified business days

**Prorations:** Loan interest, property taxes, insurance, rentals prorated through Closing Date; security deposits transferred to Buyer

**Condition of Property:**
- Seller warrants house (will/will not) be in same condition at closing
- Mechanical systems (will/will not) be in good condition
- Appliances (will/will not) be in working condition
- Property delivery options: Uninhabited, per existing lease, or seller leaseback

**Closing Date:** To be filled in (on or before specified date)

**Termination Option:** Buyer may pay Option Fee for unrestricted right to terminate within Option Period

**Defaults:**
- Buyer default: Seller receives earnest money as liquidated damages
- Seller default: Buyer may enforce specific performance

**Access:** Seller grants Buyer access for inspections; Buyer may add lockbox; Buyer may make repairs if vacant

**Special Provisions:** "Liability shall be limited to the property itself and shall not extend beyond."

## Notes
- "and/or assigns" language allows wholesale assignment
- This is Webber Investment Homes LLC's standard acquisition contract
- Used for both wholesale assignments and direct acquisitions
