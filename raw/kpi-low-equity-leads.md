# KPI — Low Equity Leads

**Description:** KPI tracker for low-equity lead campaigns and conversion rates.

## Notes
- Tracking spreadsheet for low-equity lead campaigns
- Detailed daily activity data similar to KPI Outreach sheet
- Agents tracked: Octavia, Mostafa, Hafez (Kwin/Hafez are from earlier periods)
- Tracks: Total Actions, Sent to Underwriter, Qualified Offers Sent, Offer Accepted, Text No Response
- Also tracks offer pipeline: New Offers, Multi Offers, Disqualified, Follow-Up Due, Overdue

## KPI Grade Scale
- Win → 90%+ disposition rate
- Momentum → 85-89%
- Average → standard performance
- Working → below average
