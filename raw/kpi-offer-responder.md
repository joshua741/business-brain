# KPI Offer Responder

**Description:** KPI tracker for offer response rates in acquisition campaigns.

## Tracking Framework

Daily KPI tracking for offer response pipeline — tracks beginning and end of day state for each bucket.

## Columns Tracked
- Total Actions
- Sent To Underwriter
- New Total Agent Offers Sent
- Offer Accepted
- Text (No Response) < 3
- GRADE
- Date/Agent
- New Offers (beginning/end of day, sent during day)
- Multi Offers tracking
- Underwriter Disqualified
- Offer Follow-Up Due
- New Due Today
- Due Today (task tracking)
- Task Overdue
- Offer Accepted (end of day)
- Text No Response (end of day)

## Productivity/Grade Framework
- Total Actions goal: 45+
- Sent to Underwriter target: daily
- Grades: Working / Average / Momentum / Win
