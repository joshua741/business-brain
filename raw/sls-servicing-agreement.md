# Southern Loan Servicing — Contract/Noteholder Servicing Agreement

**Document:** SLS Servicing Agreement (16 pages + Appendices)
**Servicer:** Southern Loan Servicing (SLS), 2325 Manhattan Blvd, Harvey, Louisiana (Jefferson Parish)
**Licensed by:** Louisiana Office of Financial Institutions as Bond For Deed escrow agent

## Purpose
Used to service owner-financed contracts (seller finance, subject-to, bond for deed) where SLS collects monthly payments from buyers/borrowers and disburses to sellers/lenders.

## Servicing Responsibilities (SLS)
1. Provide monthly account statements to Borrower
2. Collect all payments, escrows, and fees
3. Notify Seller/Buyer of late/no payment events
4. Disburse funds to appropriate parties (principal, interest, taxes, insurance)
5. Issue payoff demands and beneficiary statements
6. Issue annual 1098-INT to Lenders and 1099-INT to Borrowers
7. Answer borrower inquiries

## Payment Handling
- SLS deducts fees before disbursing to lenders
- Payment order: (1) SLS fees, (2) Mortgage company P&I + escrow, (3) Seller P&I, (4) Other charges
- ACH debits should be set up to fund no later than the 20th of preceding month
- No re-deposit of returned items — must use certified funds
- Short payments may be applied to reserves at SLS discretion

## Key Contract Terms
- Seller remains responsible for underlying mortgage payments even if Buyer does not pay
- 30-day written notice required for cancellation by either party
- Governed by Louisiana law
- SLS is NOT a collection agency or property manager
