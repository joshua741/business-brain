# Team Goals KPIs

**Description:** Team KPI goals document for tracking outreach and business targets.

## Outbound Lead Conversion Benchmarks

| Stage | Target Rate |
|---|---|
| Lead to Net | 80% |
| Net Lead to Appointment | 20% |
| Appointment to Offer | 85% |
| Offer to Contract | 15% |
| Contract to Close | 75% |

## Inbound Lead Conversion Benchmarks

| Stage | Target Rate |
|---|---|
| Lead to Net | 65% |
| Net Lead to Appointment | 60% |
| Appointment to Offer | 85% |
| Offer to Contract | 20% |
| Contract to Close | 75% |

## Notes
- These are the funnel conversion targets for the wholesale acquisition pipeline
- Inbound converts at higher rates than outbound (higher intent leads)
- Appointment to offer and contract-to-close rates are the same for both channels
- Used to calculate lead volume needed to achieve deal targets
