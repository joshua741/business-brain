# NEPQ Aged Leads Script

**Description:** NEPQ (New Era of Problem-Centered Questioning) sales script for working aged real estate leads.

## Script Framework

### Connection Questions (Takes focus off you, puts it on them)
- "Hey [Seller] can you hear me? Hey it's just [Name], with [Company Name]. I'm not even sure if we should be talking. It looks like you reached out to us [timeline] through [Facebook] about [address] to see what your home might be worth. And if you'd be open to moving from it in the future. Is that right?"
- "Did you end up doing anything with this property?"
- "Do you guys still own [address]?"

### Status Frame (Position yourself as authority)
- "Yeah that's a pretty common reason why a lot of people work with us [repeat their reason]"
- "This first call is pretty basic really. It's really more for us to find out a little bit more about the property... just to see if we can do any good for ya because honestly, with how the market is we are not buying EVERY property. Does that make sense?"

### Situation Questions
- Homeowner: "Oh! Where are you headed next? If Unsure: Well we don't want to just kick you out in the streets! But, seriously the investors are going to want to know. If we end up buying it, what's the plan?"
- Investor: "Are there tenants there now? What are they paying? Are they on a lease? Are they paying on time?"
- Vacancy: "How long has it been vacant? What are you doing now to keep up with the maintenance?"

### Problem Awareness (How are problems impacting them)
- "So besides [pain point], what was it that came up that made you want to look at possibly moving on from this house… now?"
- "What was the triggering point?"
- "What's the rationale behind wanting to possibly sell vs listing with a realtor?"

### Create a Gap (Get them to articulate the feeling)
- "You said that [situation]... could you clarify that with me?"
- "How long has that been going on?"
- "Hold up.. So you had to deal with.. [Rational Impact]"
- "How has this impacted you?"
- Commitment: "So it sounds like it's important for you to do something about this then?"

### Solution Awareness
- **Time:** "So ideally, when would you like to [repeat back what they're after]?"
- **Objection Prevention:** "And before talking with me, just to save us some time, what have you tried to do so far to [take care of problem]?"

### Deal Killers
- "Are you needing to stay in the property for 30-60 days after or are you hoping to start moving during the closing?"
- "Who else would want to know that you're selling this house?"

### Future Pacing
- "So let's say that you're able to sell the home and you're no longer dealing with [problem]... what are you looking forward to... the most... about that next chapter of your life?"

### NEEDS (Price)
- "So what do you feel like you need in your pocket after all is said and done so that you can [desired state]?"
- Anchor: "Well I'm going to do everything I can to get every penny of that $[amount]... Now let's say for whatever reason all of the offers come back lower... like $[80% anchor]... should I just tell them to kick rocks or would you still be able to [desired state]?"

### Consequence
- "Have you considered what would happen if you don't do anything about this?"
- "What would happen if we keep pushing this off like we have been?"

### Stress Test (Pre-close)
- "So let's say we can get you $[amount] so that you no longer have to deal with [problem]... and we can do that in the next __ days all while taking care of [their concerns]... What would be your biggest concern at that point?"

### Transition to Close
- "Here's what I'll do from here, I'll send this to my team and outline everything that we spoke about... and if it looks like something we can help with... I'll come back and walk you through the next steps... would that be helpful?"

### Presentation
- Present offer at 90-95% of actual offer to anchor
- "Ok so after reviewing this with our investors it looks like the best offer you could get so that way you could [desired state] is $____. Now that's what you'll walk away with in your pocket minus your mortgage... all while doing this in the next __ days... Do you feel like that could be the answer for you?"

### Market Max Program (If NO on price)
- Present as alternative if seller needs more money
- Essentially a hybrid listing/investor approach: dedicated team, 90-day contract (vs 6-month realtor), thousands of own buyers + MLS, no cost to seller
