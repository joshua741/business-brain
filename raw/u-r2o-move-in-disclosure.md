Payment and Eviction Policy

We take our business seriously and are dedicated to helping you succeed while you are leasing with us. Timely payments are essential to the program. Here's a straightforward overview of our process for handling late payments:

- There is a 3-day grace period after the due date.
- After the grace period, a late fee of $50 per day will be applied until the total late fees reach 12% of your gross monthly rent.
- If payment is 7 days late, a Notice to Quit will be mailed, posted on your door, and delivered to you, giving you an additional 7 days to resolve the issue.
- If the delinquency reaches 14 days, we will file for eviction and add the eviction costs to your account, plus an administrative fee of 50% of those costs.
- You will have until the court date and any time before to catch up on all payments, including additional costs.
- If not resolved by the court date, a judge may rule for you to vacate the property. At that point, we will coordinate with a constable, sheriff, movers, a roll-off dumpster company, and a locksmith to facilitate the transition.

We outline this process transparently because we believe in accountability and want to support your long-term success. By staying current on payments, you avoid these steps and move closer to owning your home.
