# Credit Repair Email Seller Finance

**Summary**: Email template and outreach script for partnering with credit repair specialists — offering referral fees (first month's mortgage payment) for clients placed into seller finance deals.

## Email Template (Tab 1)

Hello (Insert Credit Specialist FIRST NAME),

I would like to pay you a referral for the credit repair clients that you have that are looking to finance a home.

My business is that I offer individuals that are looking to own a home an alternative way to finance without going through the bank. This is called Seller Finance.

I find the homes in areas that your clients are looking for, then we finance these properties to your clients. Their name will be on the deed, showing that they own the home, while I will own a note (a note is just an agreement that your clients will pay me) the same way the bank does.

Unlike the bank I do not care about their credit score as much as I do their income. As long as your clients can afford the home and have the money for the down payment, they are approved.

**Interest Rate Scale:**
- 10% Down Payment = 8% Interest Rate
- 9% Down Payment = 9% Interest Rate
- 8% Down Payment = 10% Interest Rate
- 7% Down Payment = 11% Interest Rate
- 6% Down Payment = 12% Interest Rate
- 5% Down Payment = 13% Interest Rate

We offer 30 year terms. Properties are sourced from the MLS — renovated and ready to move in.

**Referral:** For every client I close with, I will pay you their first month's mortgage payment as a referral fee as soon as we close.

Referral link: https://myspeedycashoffer.com/sellerfinanceform

## Phone Script (Tab 2)

**You**: Hey, is this (Name of company)?

**You**: Excellent, so I wanted to ask if you repair clients credit who are looking to finance a home.

**You**: So my company is actually looking to work with your clients. We offer homes for Seller Finance that's not based on credit score. As long as they have a job, can afford the payments and the amount down they are approved. Do you know much about this?

**If they don't understand:**
So.. Seller financing is when the person selling a home lets the buyer pay them directly, instead of the buyer getting a loan from the bank. The buyer makes regular payments to the seller until the house is fully paid off. It's like borrowing money from a friend, but for a house. Is that a bit more clear?

**Once understood:**

Your clients are the exact people that we work with and I wanted to see whether you can refer your clients over to us. If we close with one of them we pay the first month's mortgage payment over to you. For instance: If the client's first month's payment is $1900, this is what we pay you as a referral as soon as we close.

Our Seller Finance Homes are different in a few ways:
1. All homes are sourced from the MLS (on the market) — in great condition
2. We give the client the opportunity to choose their interest rate
3. Unlike other Seller Finance companies we mimic bank terms and give your clients 30 year notes

So if you could find out what their availability looks like we can speak with them as soon as today or tomorrow if possible! How soon can I give you a callback?"
