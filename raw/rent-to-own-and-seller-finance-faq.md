## Program Overview

At Rent2OwnCribs.com, the Rent-to-Own and Seller Financing programs empower aspiring homeowners to achieve ownership without traditional bank financing hurdles.

### Rent-to-Own Program

**What is Rent-to-Own?**
A lease agreement giving you the option to buy the home later. An option fee (deposit) locks in the purchase price and gives you exclusive purchase rights. Typically structured for 1-year terms with annual renewal.

**Key Facts:**
- Deposit: $3,500–$5,000 depending on home price. Applied toward final purchase price.
- Monthly payments: NOT credited toward purchase price (option fee is)
- Purchase price: Locked in at signing
- Minimum credit: 520+
- Minimum income: 2.5x the monthly rent (gross)
- No foreclosure last 5 years, no bankruptcy last 2 years, no evictions/judgments

**Equitable Interest:** You hold legal right to buy; seller cannot sell to anyone else while you're in compliance.

**Qualifying for Seller Finance from RTO (based on on-time payment %):**
- Months 0–12: Not eligible
- Months 13–24: Need 80%+ on-time; deposit $3,000 (0 lates), $4,000 (1-2 lates), $5,000 (3-4 lates), ineligible (5+ lates)
- Months 25–36: Deposit waived (0-3 lates), $3,000 (4 lates), $5,000 (5-7 lates), ineligible (8+ lates)

### Seller Financing Program

**What is Seller Financing?**
Direct ownership from day one. 30-year fully amortized note. Title transfers at closing. No bank involved.

**Key Facts:**
- Minimum credit: 600+ (560-599 with 3.5x income)
- Minimum income: 3x monthly payment (gross)
- Prepayment penalty: 5% of remaining balance if refinanced/sold within first 5 years (waived after 60 months)
- Cannot transfer deed or wrap the mortgage while loan is active (due-on-sale clause)
- Serviced by third-party company

## Detailed FAQ Highlights

**Do rent payments build equity?** No — only the option fee is credited toward the purchase price.

**Can I buy early before lease ends?** Yes, at any time during the agreement.

**Who pays for repairs in RTO?** The tenant-buyer is responsible — treated like an owner from day one.

**Can I make changes to the home?** Minor cosmetic changes with approval. Major/structural changes must be approved in writing. Neutral colors only.

**Pets allowed?** Usually yes. Up to 3 pets. $150/pet fee + $25/month pet rent. Restricted breeds: Pit Bulls, Rottweilers, Staffordshire Terriers. No dogs over 50 lbs (per property rules). Service animals exempt with documentation.

**Renters insurance required?** Yes — required during lease period.

**Is the deposit refundable?** No — it's non-refundable but credited toward purchase if you buy.

**Can I sublease?** No.

**Do we report to credit bureaus?** No — but payment history is visible to future lenders.

**Cash purchases accepted?** No — all properties are exclusively RTO or Seller Finance.

**Can I have a home inspection before signing?** Yes — always recommended.

**Split down payment over time?** No — full down payment required before move-in.

## Program Comparison

| Feature | Rent-to-Own | Seller Financing |
|---|---|---|
| Ownership Type | Equitable title (option to buy) | Full legal title at closing |
| Monthly Payments Applied To | Rent only (no equity build) | Principal, interest, taxes, insurance |

| Term | 1-year (renewable) | 30-year amortized |
| Purchase Price | Fixed; adjustable post-term if unpurchased | Locked at closing |
| Renovation Approval | Required | None — full freedom |
| Min Credit Score | 520 (530+ with 3x income) | 600 (560+ with 3.5x income) |
| Income Multiple | 2.5–3x monthly rent | 3–3.5x monthly payment |
| Prepayment Penalty | None | 5% first 5 years, waived after 60 months |
| Cash Purchase Allowed | No | No |
