# Marketing Campaigns

**Description:** Marketing campaigns tracker — shared spreadsheet for campaign performance.

## Campaign Buckets

### Bucket 1 — Equity
- High Equity lists
- Unknown Equity lists
- Unknown Sale Date lists
- Free and Clear lists
- All on unique lists (0-1 list count)

### Bucket 2 — Stacked
- Properties on 2+ lists
- Numbers adjusted to include both mailing and property
- Exclude: Buyers tag, Leads tag, Money Bags list

### Bucket 3 — Vacant
- Properties on 1+ lists
- Vacant property: YES
- Exclude: Buyers, Money Bags

### Bucket 4 — Ouchies (Deal Machine / Tax Delinquent)
- Deal Machine list
- Tax Delinquent list
- Probate list
