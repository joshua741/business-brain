WHEREAS, the Landlord and Tenant(s) entered into a Lease Agreement dated ____________ (the "Lease") for the property located at ____________ (the "Property"); and

WHEREAS, the parties desire to amend the Lease to require automatic bank drafts for rent payment and to impose a fee for failure to maintain such enrollment;

---

## Section 1 — Automatic Bank Draft Requirement

Tenant(s) agree to enroll in and maintain automatic bank drafts (ACH / electronic funds transfer) from their designated bank account for all monthly rent obligations. Tenant shall provide accurate banking information upon execution or as requested. Automatic drafts shall commence on ____________ and continue for the full lease term, including renewals.

## Section 2 — Fee for Non-Compliance

If Tenant removes, cancels, or fails to maintain valid banking information for automatic drafts, a monthly **Non-Compliance Fee equal to 5% of gross monthly rent** shall be assessed for each full or partial month the auto-draft is inactive. This fee is due with the next rent payment and is in addition to any other late fees or remedies under the Lease.

## Section 3 — Reinstatement

Tenant may reinstate automatic bank drafts at any time by providing updated banking information. The Non-Compliance Fee ceases to accrue beginning the first full month after successful reinstatement.

## Section 4 — Integration and Effect on Lease

This Addendum is incorporated into and made part of the Lease. In the event of conflict between this Addendum and the Lease, this Addendum prevails on its subject matter. All other Lease terms remain in full force.

## Section 5 — Governing Law

State of Texas, without regard to conflict of laws principles.

## Section 6 — Severability

If any provision is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in full force.
