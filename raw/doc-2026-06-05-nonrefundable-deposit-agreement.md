## Key Terms

**Purpose:** Reserves the property for RTO or SF consideration. NOT a security deposit, rent payment, mortgage payment, down payment, or guarantee of possession.

**Non-refundable unless:** Property becomes unavailable for reasons not caused by the applicant/buyer.

**Forfeiture triggers:**
- Failure to make required rent/mortgage, remaining deposit/down payment, or any related cost within 2 days of due date (or 7-day advance written/verbal notice)
- Failure to demonstrate sufficient income (at landlord/lender discretion)
- Decision or inability to proceed for any reason
- Refusal to sign required documents
- Fraudulent information on application
- Refusal to provide ACH info for automatic drafts
- (SF Only) Inability to provide proof of home insurance within 7 days of closing

**Move-in timing:**
- Move-in on the 25th → full rent/mortgage due that date
- Move-in before or after 25th → prorated payment required

**Due date changes:**
- Extensions (pushback) → written amendment signed by both parties
- Acceleration (moved forward) → seller/lender discretion, no buyer signature required

**Property availability:** If buyer fails to submit first payment or remaining deposit within 2 days of due date, property may be re-listed and holding fee forfeited.

**(SF Only):** Closing costs, attorney drafting fees, servicing fees ≈ 1% of purchase price (separate from down payment).

## Disclosure
Buyer acknowledges full understanding, voluntary entry, no coercion, no liability to seller/lender for damages or losses. Initials required.
