## Service Pricing

| Service Type | Notes |
|---|---|
| Standard Clean (Recurring) | Base service |
| Deep Clean | Premium, higher margin |
| Move In/Out Clean | Premium, higher margin |
| Initial Clean (new client) | Entry point |
| Commercial (per visit) | Separate pricing |

**Pricing Philosophy:**
- Price per JOB, never hourly
- Quote based on sq ft + condition + frequency
- Recurring clients get 10–15% discount vs one-time
- Always include supply costs in price

## Profit First Allocation (Phase 1 — under $250K/yr)

| Account | Allocation % | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Operating Expenses | 52% | Pay cleaners, supplies, gas, fixed costs |
| Tax Reserve | 15% | Federal + SE tax reserve |
| Owner Pay | 20% | Joshua + Oralia distributions |
| Profit | 5% | Quarterly distribution (50% take, 50% hold) |
| Goal Sinking Funds | 8% | Camp Fund first, then House Fund |

**Allocation Schedule:** 10th and 25th of every month

**Goal Waterfall:**
- If Camp Fund < $2,200: ALL 8% → Camp Fund
- If Camp Fund = $2,200 (funded): ALL 8% → House Fund + any Owner Pay Joshua directs
