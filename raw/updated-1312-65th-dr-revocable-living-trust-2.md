## Purpose
Created for the benefit of Beneficiaries after the death of both Grantors. Primary goal: protect value of the Property (1312 65th Dr, Lubbock TX 79412). Secondary goal: generate income and growth at reasonable risk.

## Property (Schedule A)
- 1312 65th Dr, Lubbock TX 79412

## Key Trust Provisions
- **Revocable** during lifetime of Grantors — can be amended or revoked by written instrument
- **After death of both Grantors:** 100 shares distributed to Dillon Ford (Lewisville TX); if Dillon Ford does not survive → reverts to Webber Wealth Holdings LLC
- **Distributions during lifetime:** Trustee distributes income/principal as Grantors request
- **Trustee powers:** Same rights as property owner; employ experts; retain/sell property; invest; borrow; maintain, settle, or defend claims
- **Trustee compensation:** Non-beneficiary Trustee receives reasonable compensation; Trustee who is also Beneficiary serves without compensation
- **Spendthrift clause:** Beneficiaries cannot transfer/assign/encumber assets prior to Final Distribution
- **Termination:** When Property is exhausted; or when value falls below $50,000
- **Homestead exemption:** Grantors maintain right to possess/inhabit residence rent-free for lifetime
- **Tax ID:** Grantors' Social Security Numbers during their lifetime; EIN applied for after death

## Context
This trust structure is part of the creative finance purchase of 1312 65th Dr from Dillon Ford via seller finance at $435,000 (6.38% interest, $2,310.94/mo, 35-year term maturing May 13, 2060). The trust holds the property while the deed of trust secures the loan to Dillon Ford (beneficiary/lender).
