## Financing Consideration Criteria

Financing eligibility is primarily determined by the tenant's on-time payment percentage. A late payment is defined as any payment made after the five (5) day grace period.

**0-12 Months:** Not eligible. Late payments in this period are included in total count.

**13-24 Months:**
- 0 late payments → Eligible with $3,000 deposit
- 1-2 late payments → Eligible with $4,000 deposit
- 3-4 late payments → Eligible with $5,000 deposit (on-time % must be above 80%)
- 5+ late payments → Not eligible

**25-36 Months:**
- 0-3 late payments → Deposit waived
- 4 late payments → Eligible with $3,000 deposit
- 5-7 late payments → Eligible with $5,000 deposit (on-time % must be above 80%)
- 8+ late payments → Not eligible

If at any point on-time payment percentage falls below 80%, financing consideration is voided until the percentage is restored.

The Deposit Credit will be solely applied towards the Financed Amount. No additional deposits will be considered. Landlord reserves the right to approve or deny financing regardless of status.
