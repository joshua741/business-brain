# Fillable Revocable Living Trust

**Document:** Generic fillable revocable living trust template
**Source:** legaltemplates.net
**Governing Law:** State of _____________ (blank — to be filled in)

## Structure

Blank-field template covering all standard revocable living trust provisions. Key sections:

**PART I — Trust Name:** Grantor names the trust "[Grantor Name] Revocable Living Trust"

**PART II — Trust Property:** Assets transferred listed in Schedule A

**PART III — Purpose:** Checkboxes: manage/control assets, distribute on death, other

**PART IV — Funding:** All assets in Schedule A become "Trust Property"

**PART V — Amendment or Revocation:** Grantor may revoke or amend in writing during lifetime; trust becomes irrevocable on death

**PART VI — Trustee:** Options for Grantor as trustee, third-party trustee, or co-trustees; Successor Trustee and Backup Successor Trustee slots; compensation options (none / reasonable / fixed dollar)

**PART VII — Trustee's Powers:** Full Uniform Trust Code powers enumerated with checkboxes for exceptions; includes powers to collect, sell, exchange, deposit, borrow, continue business interests, vote securities, manage real property, lease, grant options, insure, invest, settle claims, pay taxes, make loans, delegate duties

**PART VIII — Change of Trustee:** Trustee may resign with 30 days notice; Grantor may remove by written notice

**PART IX — Distributions During Grantor's Lifetime:** Trustee distributes income/principal as Grantor requests; if incapacitated, Trustee distributes reasonable support amount

**PART X — Death of Grantor:** Trustee distributes Trust Property per trust terms after resolving all obligations

**PART XI — Specific Gifts:** Optional Schedule B for named bequests

**PART XII — Children's Subtrust:** Options for one child or multiple children; distributions for support, maintenance, education; terminates when child reaches specified age/milestone

**PART XII (B) — Beneficiaries:** Residual distribution percentages listed in Schedule D; beneficiary must survive Grantor by 30 days

**PART XIII — Pet Trust:** Optional dollar amount set aside for care of pets

**PART XIV — Miscellaneous:**
- Uniform Transfers to Minors Act applies
- Property passes subject to existing mortgages/liens
- Annual accounting to beneficiaries
- Spendthrift clause — beneficiaries cannot assign or encumber interest before Final Distribution
- Homestead exemption — Grantor retains right to inhabit residence rent-free for lifetime
- Trustee may present certificate/abstract of trust as proof of trust existence
- Severability and governing law clauses
