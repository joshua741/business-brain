## Key Provisions (from partial extraction)

This is the residential lease agreement template used for standard rental placements in the Webber portfolio.

Key provisions include sublease addendum language:

1. **Permission to sublease:** Tenant granted permission to sublease for duration of lease term
2. **Duration:** Sublease period begins 60 days after signing (allows tenant to purchase appliances/furniture, register with city); ends one year after; additional periods require written consent
3. **Restrictions:** No more than 1 sublessee at one time without prior written consent
4. **Local laws compliance:** Tenant must comply with vacation rental regulations (including Chapter 28 Houston Code of Ordinances as example); obtain permits/licenses; indemnify Landlord from violations
5. **Liability/Insurance:** Tenant must obtain liability insurance for sublease activities; solely responsible for injuries during sublease
6. **Damage Responsibility:** Tenant responsible for damages; notify Landlord within 24 hours; Tenant pays up to $1,000/incident; Major ticket items Landlord's responsibility
7. **Professional Cleaning:** Tenant ensures professional cleaning after each guest stay; property maintained in sell-ready condition

**Major Ticket Items (Landlord's Responsibility):**
HVAC, roof, foundation, electrical wiring/panels, plumbing, water heater, appliances, windows, doors, flooring, bathroom fixtures, kitchen cabinets/countertops, lighting fixtures, garage door, fencing, security system, deck/patio, gutters, driveway, attic insulation, septic system, sprinkler system
