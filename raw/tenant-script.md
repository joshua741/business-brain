# Tenant Script

**Summary**: Phone/outreach script for prospective tenant conversations — covers intro, pet qualification, occupancy checks, and next steps (application or tour).

**Sources**: Google Drive — Tenant Script (1mKdc8ryJQBv0_M7jsjDburCigRrewe5sps0Hhc3v894)

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

## Intro

Hello and yes this unit is still available. How soon are you looking to move in?

## Follow-up

Ok and are you moving in with children, spouse or pets?

---

## If Yes to Pets

**Are these service animals?**

**Not a Service Animal:**
- How many pets do you have and how many pounds are they?
  - FAIL: More than 2 pets or any pet over 50 lbs
  - PASS: Go to step "Pets Pass"

**If Yes to Service Animals:**
- Would you happen to have documentation for this?
  - No Documentation: Go to Question b (same as non-service animal)
  - Yes Documentation: Go to "Pets Pass"

**Pets Pass:**
- So there is a $199 per pet deposit and a $20 per month fee for pets. Is this ok for you?
  - OK: Go to step f (final step)
  - Not OK: "Ok I apologize well is there anything that I can clear up for you or is this not able to work for you?"

---

## If Yes to Children and Spouse

- So how many people are you looking to be in the unit with you?
  - FAIL: More than 2 other people in the unit (including children). Max = 2 people per bedroom plus a child.

---

## Pass — Pets / # of People / Documented Service Animal / OR No Children or Spouse

Ok so the next steps would be either sending you over an application or scheduling you a tour. Which one can I help you with?
