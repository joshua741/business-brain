# Ultimate Agent Outreach Script for Wholesalers (2025)
**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mJhkCoULkgU   **Duration:** 00:09:44

## Summary
This video details a strategy for real estate investors, specifically wholesalers, to acquire "ugly houses" or off-market properties using a free lead generation method. The speaker emphasizes working with real estate agents who provide "pocket listings" – properties not yet on the market. He shares a specific script designed for both cold calling and texting agents, highlighting its effectiveness in generating leads with a high conversion rate (12-20 leads per deal) and a low cost ($100-$150 per deal).

The core of the strategy revolves around consistent, high-volume outreach (300-500 texts/calls daily) and building relationships with agents, even if they don't immediately have properties. The video provides a detailed breakdown of the script, including how to initiate contact, follow up, and handle common objections from agents. It also touches on the current market dynamics, noting that a buyer's heavy market with adjusting property prices creates leverage for investors and opportunities for agents seeking to move properties. The speaker encourages viewers to download a free "Build a Business Guide" which contains the script and further details.

## Key Takeaways
-   **Focus on Off-Market Properties:** Prioritize "pocket listings" from real estate agents as they offer significantly better conversion rates (12-20 leads per deal) compared to on-market properties (41-62 leads per deal).
-   **Leverage Real Estate Agents:** Agents can be a primary source of free leads for wholesaling, especially for properties needing work that haven't hit the market.
-   **High Volume and Consistency are Crucial:** Success in lead generation requires consistent daily outreach, sending 300-500 text messages or making calls to agents.
-   **Build Relationships:** Even if agents don't have properties immediately, building a relationship can lead to future deals, especially in a buyer's market where agents are looking for solutions.
-   **Use a Structured Script:** A well-designed script for initial contact and objection handling provides confidence and guides conversations effectively.
-   **Low Cost of Lead Generation:** The primary cost for this method is minimal, mainly the cost of texting, making it highly profitable (75-78% profit margin).
-   **Buyer's Market Advantage:** Current market conditions (adjusting prices, higher interest rates) create leverage for buyers and motivate agents to work with investors who can close deals.

## Frameworks / SOPs
-   **Cold Call/Text Script for Real Estate Agents:** A detailed, step-by-step script is provided for initiating contact with real estate agents, following up, and handling various objections.
    1.  **Initial Contact (Assuming Prior Outreach):** Start by saying, "Hey, I reached out about a month ago and we hadn't gotten a chance to connect yet. It was about properties that needed some work." (For text, adapt to "Hey, just checking back in, see if anything's coming up that needs some work. This is [Your Name] by the way.")
    2.  **Handling "Don't Remember/Don't Have Anything":** Respond with, "Great, I was just following up with you, I wanted to see if you had anything coming up that hasn't hit the market yet." Emphasize off-market properties.
    3.  **If No Off-Market Properties:** Ask, "How often do you run across properties that need some work or aren't market ready?"
    4.  **General Response for No Properties:** Say, "Ok that makes sense. Well we buy all kinds of properties including multifamily and commercial. Honestly, I try to set myself apart from all the other investors by putting a voice to the name. Do you know of anyone that may have something coming soon? We would be happy to have you represent us in the transaction." (Adjust for text by removing "putting a voice to the name").
    5.  **Handling Objections:** The script includes specific responses for common objections such as:
        *   "Do you have or can you provide proof of funds?"
        *   "What is your buy box or your buying criteria?"
        *   "Are you an investor or are you a wholesaler?"
        *   "What company do you work for?"
        *   "How did you get my number to contact?"
        *   "Can you tell me how an agent gets paid in this situation/deal?"
        *   "I only send deals to people that sign my brokerage agreement."
        *   "I am a flipper/investor myself."
        *   "What properties are you interested in? What property are you calling about? Which property?"
        *   "When they don't know who you are."
        *   "Are you an agent?"
        *   "Remove me from your list."
        Each objection response is designed to answer the question and then pivot back to asking about potential off-market properties.
    6.  **Follow-up:** If an agent doesn't have anything but allows a follow-up, categorize them as a "Tier 3 agent" and schedule a follow-up.
-   **Three-Part Process for Closing Deals with Real Estate Agents:** The video mentions this as a broader framework, with the script being the first part. (Further details on parts 2 and 3 are not provided in this video).
-   **Build a Business Guide:** A free resource mentioned at the end of the video that contains the full script and additional information for implementing this strategy.
