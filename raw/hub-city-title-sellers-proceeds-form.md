# Hub City Title — Seller Proceeds Form

**Description:** Hub City Title seller proceeds disbursement instruction form.

## Disbursement Options

Seller authorizes Escrow Agent (Hub City Title) to disburse net proceeds via:

1. **Wire Transfer** — Attach voided check; note: return wire fee up to $35 for incorrect info; bank may charge incoming wire fee
   - Fields: Bank name, ABA Routing #, Customer Account Name, Account #

2. **Pick Up Check** — Call Seller at [phone]

3. **Mail Check to Seller** at address shown

4. **Overnight Check** — Overnight delivery fee deducted from proceeds; physical address required (no P.O. box)

5. **Transfer to Purchase Transaction** — Must match buyer/seller names; transfer to specified GF #

6. **Split Proceeds** — Multiple payees with individual instructions; total must equal net proceeds

## Seller Information Required
- Name or Entity
- Signature
- Social Security Number or EIN
- Mailing Address
- Phone Number
- Email Address

## Notes
- Standard form used by Hub City Title for all closings
- Sellers must authorize disbursement method in writing before closing
- Used in conjunction with HUD settlement statements for Webber portfolio acquisitions
