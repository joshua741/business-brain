**Description:** Webber Wealth Holdings LLC branded assignment of contract template for wholesale transactions.
**Seller Entity:** Webber Wealth Holdings LLC

## Full Contract Text

CONTRACT ASSIGNMENT OF CONTRACT/INTEREST FOR PURCHASE OF RESIDENCE AND CONTROLLED BUSINESS OR DISCLOSURE OTHER REAL AND ESTATE HOLD HARMLESS

THIS CONTRACT is made on the ________________ by and between Webber Wealth Holdings LLC (Seller) and between ______________________________ (Buyer).

**LEGAL DESCRIPTION:** The parties agree to allow the Title Company to insert the correct legal description for ______________________________.

**PURCHASE AND SALE:** The Seller agrees to sell and Buyer agrees to buy the property located at: ______________________________ together with all fixtures, landscaping, improvements, and appurtenances, all being hereinafter collectively referred to as the "Property".

**PURCHASE PRICE:** $ _______________.

**TERMS:** _____________________________________________________________________________.

**CLOSING COSTS:** $ _______________.

**PROPERTY CONDITION:** Buyer agrees to purchase Property in "As-Is" condition. No repairs will be made by seller or owner(s) of record.

**TITLE INSURANCE COMPANY:** _______________________________________________________________.

**DEPOSIT:** Upon acceptance, Buyer will place in escrow an earnest money deposit of $ ______________ with title/escrow company which will be part of cash paid to the Seller when title transfers. This deposit will be returned to the Buyer if title does not transfer in accordance with this agreement.

**CLOSING:** Closing will take place on or before ________________. Subject to a 90 day period in which the SELLER shall be permitted to clear any title problems. Buyer default — deposit paid to Seller as liquidated damages.

**CLEAR MARKETABLE TITLE:** Seller agrees to deliver a good and marketable or insurable owner's title free and clear of all encumbrances. Buyer shall pay all closing costs and title fees.

**RELEASE OF EARNEST MONEY AUTHORIZATION:** If BUYER fails to close by ____________ BUYER authorizes escrow agent/title company to release earnest money to SELLER.

**PRORATIONS:** Real property taxes prorated based on current year's tax. Rents prorated as of date title transfers.

**INVESTMENT DISCLOSURES:** Buyer warrants purchasing for investment, not personal residence. Contract is NOT assignable. Buyer may not resell or market the property without express written consent from Seller prior to closing.

**POSSESSION:** Possession given to purchaser at time of closing subject to any leases.

**SELLER ASSIGNMENT CAPABILITY:** Parties may do an assignment of Seller's contract with current owner if Seller has not yet closed. Buyer's total purchase price remains the same after assignment fee.

**ACCEPTANCE:** Binding when accepted by Seller and signed by both parties with receipted Earnest Money Deposit.

**TIME IS OF THE ESSENCE:** All deadlines are strict and absolute. If closing does not occur by Closing Date, Agreement is automatically terminated and Seller retains earnest money as liquidated damages.

**BUYER EXTENSION REQUESTS:** Extension requires per diem penalty of the greater of $200.00 or 1/10 of 1% of purchase price per calendar day.

**PROHIBITION:** Agreement establishes a prohibition against transfer, conveyance or encumbrance of Property.

**MARKETING SERVICES AND/OR SALE COMMISSIONS:** ______________________ shall be paid from Seller proceeds $ ____________ upon closing and funding.

## Cash Sale Addendum

**NO FINANCING CONTINGENCY:** Cash buyers only. If using hard money lender or IRA, proof of funds required at acceptance.

**SPECIFIC PERFORMANCE AND/OR LIS PENDENS:** Buyer expressly waives right of specific performance and right to record a lis pendens.

**EXCLUSIVITY:** Seller reserves right to continue offering Property for sale until formal written acceptance and Earnest Deposit delivered to Title Company within 24 hours.

**PROPERTY TAXES:** No adjustments between Seller and Purchaser after closing.

**CLOSING COSTS:** Buyer agrees to pay all normal closing costs except taxes or liens, inclusive of SELLER's property acquisition closing costs from previous owner of record.

**AUTHORITY:** Undersigned agrees they have full authority to execute this document.

AS-IS CONDITION: Closing constitutes acknowledgement that premises were accepted without representation or warranty of any kind, in present AS-IS condition based solely on Buyer's own inspection.
