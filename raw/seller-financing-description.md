## Purchase Steps

1. **Email:** Send to joshua@webberinvestmenthomes.com
   - Last 2 bank statements
   - Last 2 pay stubs
   - Photo of ID
   - Phone number
   - Mention property address and how soon you'd like to close
   - Subject: "Proof of Income - FIRST LAST - 126 Paul St, San Antonio, TX"

2. **We Contact You:** Response within 1-2 business days. Text/call 806-781-8495.

3. **Schedule Walkthrough:** Once confirmed as a good fit.

4. **Deposit & Sign:** Documents sent within 24 hours. Wire deposit within 2 business days. Home remains available to others until both complete.

5. **Pending Status:** After deposit received, submitted to title company.

6. **Meet Insurance Agent:** Title company submission triggers insurance setup.

7. **Close & Move In:** Wire down payment to title company. Sign closing documents. Official owner.

---

## Key Rules While Financed

1. The deed will be held in a Trust.
2. No second-position loans allowed on the property.

**Title Structure:** Title placed in a Trust with you as beneficiary/owner. Protects the seller financing agreement.

**When Home Goes in Your Name:**
- Option 1: Once you pay off the mortgage
- Option 2: If you refinance with another lender

**Additional Liens:** A deed restriction prevents any second loans or HELOCs while financed with us. This secures our position as lender.

**You can always:**
- Sell the home at any time
- Cash out on equity as it appreciates
