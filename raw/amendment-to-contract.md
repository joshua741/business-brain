# Amendment to Contract

**Description:** TREC contract amendment document for a real estate transaction.
**TREC No.:** 39-8 | **Promulgated by:** Texas Real Estate Commission
**Date:** 11-2-2015

## Purpose
Used to amend an existing real estate purchase contract in Texas. Checkboxes allow amendment of:

1. Sales Price (cash portion and/or financing)
2. Seller-required repairs and treatments
3. Closing date change (Paragraph 9)
4. Amount in Paragraph 12A(1)(b)
5. Cost split for lender-required repairs
6. Option fee extension (buyer pays additional Option Fee)
7. Buyer waives unrestricted right to terminate
8. Third Party Financing Addendum notice date change
9. Other modifications (factual statements and business details)

## Notes
- Standard TREC form used in Texas wholesale/creative finance transactions
- Replaces TREC No. 39-7
- Signed by Buyer and Seller with broker filling in date of final acceptance
