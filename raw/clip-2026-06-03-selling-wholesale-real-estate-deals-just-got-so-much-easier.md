## Key Takeaways

-   Sticking to a defined process and not deviating from it, even when faced with early offers, is essential for maintaining control and achieving optimal results.

## Frameworks / SOPs

**1. 5-Day Dispo Process**
This is a structured, five-day process for selling a real estate deal, designed to create demand and streamline the sales cycle.

*   **Day 0: Pre-Blast Prep**
    *   Open title with a preferred title company.
    *   Align property named ID across CRM, deal portal, and shared drive.
    *   Upload all photos, videos, and specs to the deal portal.
    *   Verify walkthrough date/time is set in the portal and calendar.
    *   Confirm CRM deal portal is fully populated – no blast until this is done.
    *   *Goal:* Get everything set before anything goes public.

*   **Day 1: Blast & Build**
    *   Send email + SMS blast to the full buyer list.
    *   Post to Facebook groups and dispo services.
    *   Log all interested replies as buyer leads in Prospecting (tag A/B/C).
    *   Dispo Manager calls A-Tier buyers personally – same day, no exceptions.
    *   *Goal:* Generate conversations and identify serious buyers, keeping copy vague to prompt replies.

*   **Day 2: Outreach**
    *   Call skip-traced active buyers in the area (not yet on list).
    *   Send basic info text and portal link to interested parties.
    *   Update all Prospects statuses in CRM.
    *   Move all interested buyers from text to a phone call.
    *   *Goal:* Grow the buyer pool and surface demand. Keep details light on text – move to the phone.

*   **Day 3: Reminders**
    *   Individually text interested buyers to confirm walkthrough attendance.
    *   Update statuses: "Attending Walkthrough" or "Not interested".
    *   Touch base with A-Tier buyers – quick personal call.
    *   *Goal:* Maximize walkthrough turnout. Get firm RSVPs and keep A-Tier buyers warm.

*   **Day 4: Walkthrough Day**
    *   Send morning reminders to all "attending" buyers with address, time, and parking.
    *   Record attendance on-site – update status to "Attended Event".
    *   Send post-walkthrough offer reminder with deadline, closing date, and EMD instructions.
    *   *Goal:* Run the walkthrough, record every attendee. Open the offer window immediately after.

*   **Day 5: Decision Day**
    *   Send Magic Question #1 text to every buyer who submitted an offer.
    *   Dispo Manager calls top 3 interested buyers with Magic Question #2.
    *   Final 30-min heads-up call to buyers #2 and #3 – last chance to improve.
    *   Select top offer at 12:00 PM sharp.
    *   Coordinate assignment/double-close docs and collect EMD receipt.
    *   Send FOMO/Thank You text to all interested – invite back-up offers via portal.
    *   Update final statuses in CRM: Purchased, back-up, not selected.
    *   *Goal:* Lock the deal by noon. Magic Questions drive best offers. Award the contract and collect EMD.

**2. 3 Core Functions for Your Business**
These are the essential functions a real estate business should automate, ideally leveraging AI, to ensure continuous lead generation and deal flow.

*   **1. General Prospecting**: Continuously looking for new "ugly houses" (distressed properties) and building a buyer's list. This involves ongoing marketing efforts to find potential sellers and buyers.
*   **2. Deal Specific Prospecting**: Once a deal is secured, using that specific property as an opportunity to attract even more buyers to the list, beyond those already identified through general prospecting.
*   **3. Co-Wholesalers/JV Partners**: Maintaining relationships with other wholesalers or joint venture partners as a worst-case scenario to help sell deals if direct buyers cannot be found. This acts as a safety net.

## Action Items
-   Join the free community mentioned in the video description to access scripts and checklists for buyer prospecting and the dispo process.
-   Utilize free tools like Zillow to identify recently remodeled or renovated properties in target areas by filtering for keywords like "remodeled" or "renovated" in listing descriptions.
-   Call listing agents of flipped properties found on Zillow to inquire if their buyers are still active and offer to send them off-market deals for a commission.
-   Consider using paid software like InvestorBase (or the internal tool mentioned) to efficiently find and gather contact information for potential buyers (flippers, landlords) in specific areas.
-   Implement the "3 Core Functions" in your real estate business:
    1.  **General Prospecting**: Continuously seek out distressed properties and build a broad buyer list.
    2.  **Deal Specific Prospecting**: Use each new deal as an opportunity to find additional, targeted buyers.
    3.  **Co-Wholesalers/JV Partners**: Establish relationships with other wholesalers or JV partners as a backup for selling deals.
-   Categorize buyers into A, B, and C tiers based on their qualification and reliability (A-tier: verified closers, B-tier: vetted with proof of funds, C-tier: interested but unvetted).
-   Implement the "5-Day Dispo Process" for every deal to create demand and ensure a structured sales approach.
-   On Day 0 (Pre-Blast Prep), ensure all deal information, photos, and walkthrough details are prepared and uploaded to a deal portal/CRM.
-   On Day 1 (Blast & Build), send out email and SMS blasts to your buyer list, prioritizing A and B-tier buyers, and post to relevant Facebook groups.
-   On Day 2 (Outreach), perform manual outreach by skip-tracing and contacting active buyers in the area who may not be on your list yet.
-   On Day 3 (Reminders), send individual text reminders to interested buyers to confirm walkthrough attendance and make personal calls to A-tier buyers.
-   On Day 4 (Walkthrough Day), send morning reminders, record attendance, and send a post-walkthrough offer reminder with a clear deadline and EMD instructions.
-   On Day 5 (Decision Day), use "Magic Questions" to drive best offers, select the top offer, coordinate closing, and send FOMO/thank you texts to all interested parties, inviting backup offers.
-   Leverage AI tools for automated outreach (texting/calling) to buyers and agents, allowing you to focus on vetting and closing.
-   Prioritize spending personal time on calls with vetted, serious buyers (A-tier and B-tier) rather than extensive manual texting.
-   Maintain clear communication and a professional process to establish credibility with buyers and ensure they prioritize your deals.
