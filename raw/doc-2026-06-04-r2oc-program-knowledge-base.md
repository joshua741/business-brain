## Who We Are
Rent2Own Cribs helps everyday people become homeowners through Rent-to-Own and Seller Financing — without needing a traditional bank loan. Solves two biggest barriers: credit qualification and large down payments. Properties sold strictly AS-IS.

## Two Paths to Ownership

**Path A — Rent-to-Own (RTO)**
Flexible entry point. Smaller initial deposit. Rents while building toward ownership. After 13+ months of consistent on-time payments, eligible to convert to Seller Financing and take title.

**Path B — Direct Seller Financing**
Higher upfront deposit, but gets title on Day 1. We act as the bank.

Both paths require monthly income ≥ 3× the monthly payment.

## Initial Deposit Formulas

Approximated Mortgage = 0.85 × Monthly Payment

**RTO Initial Deposit:**
RTO Deposit = (3 × Approximated Mortgage) + $1,500 + (0.005 × Home Price)
- 3 months mortgage — buffer for missed payments
- $1,500 — eviction-fee reserve
- 0.5% of home price — repair reserve

**Direct Seller Finance Deposit:**
SF Deposit = (6 × Approximated Mortgage) + $3,500 + $1,500 + (0.01 × Home Price)
- 6 months mortgage — larger buffer (now an owner)
- $3,500 — foreclosure-fee reserve
- $1,500 — eviction-fee reserve
- 1% of home price — repair reserve

Example ($150,000 home, $1,400/mo): RTO Initial ≈ $5,820 | Direct SF ≈ $13,640

## Secondary Deposit (RTO → Seller Finance Conversion)

Paid when RTO tenant converts to ownership. Eligibility depends on time and late-payment count.

**Formula:**
Base Fee = 0.85 × Monthly Payment
Secondary Deposit = Base Fee + (Risk % × Home Price) + $1,000 closing costs

**Eligibility & Risk %:**
| Period | Late Payments | Risk % | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–12 months | any | — | Not eligible |
| 13–24 months | 0 | 1.5% | Eligible |
| 13–24 months | 1–2 | 2.0% | Eligible |
| 13–24 months | 3–4 | 2.5% | Eligible |
| 13–24 months | 5+ | — | Not eligible |
| 25–36 months | 0–3 | 0% | $0 deposit |
| 25–36 months | 4 | 1.5% | Eligible |
| 25–36 months | 5–7 | 2.5% | Eligible |
| 25–36 months | 8+ | — | Not eligible |
| 36+ months | — | — | Reevaluated; restarts at 13–24 tier |

Must maintain 80%+ on-time payments at all times.

**Stacked Total Example:**
$140K home, $1,400/mo, 2 lates, converts at month 18:
- Initial RTO Deposit: $5,770
- Secondary Deposit: $4,990
- Total: $10,760 vs. $13,540 direct SF — saves $2,780

Best case: 25–36 months with ≤3 lates → Secondary = $0

## Qualification Requirements
1. Income — 3× monthly payment (verifiable)
2. Initial deposit — funds available for chosen path
3. Credit — NO minimum. Income-based only. No credit pull.
4. Background check — basic, for neighbor safety
5. As-Is acceptance — buyer handles all repairs from move-in

## As-Is Policy
Every property sold/rented strictly AS-IS. All repairs (plumbing, HVAC, roof, appliances, cosmetic) are tenant/buyer's responsibility from move-in. Repair reserve in initial deposit is WIH's protection, not a fund the buyer draws from.

## Process Overview
1. Browse homes at rent2owncribs.com
2. Pre-qualify (income check)
3. Qualify — verify income, choose path, confirm deposit
4. Move in — sign agreement, pay deposit, get keys

## After Move-In Lifecycle
- Months 0–12: Build payment history. Lates count against future eligibility.
- Months 13–24: First conversion window. Pay Secondary Deposit to take title.
- Months 25–36: Best window — possibly $0 Secondary Deposit.
- After 36 months: Reevaluated; eligibility tiers restart.

## Property Status Definitions
- Available (green) — Ready to move in
- Upcoming (yellow) — Coming soon; join notification list
- Pending (orange) — Under contract
- Sold (red) — No longer available

## Key Differentiators
- No bank loan needed
- No credit minimum — income-based only
- Two paths: RTO (flexible) or Direct SF (own Day 1)
- Built-in conversion path with formula-based secondary deposit
- $0 secondary deposit possible at months 25–36 with clean payment history
