# AD Spend KPIs

**Description:** KPI tracker for advertising spend across campaigns.

## Columns Tracked
- Date
- Lead Channel
- Amount Spent
- Number of Leads
- Cost Per Lead
- Deals Closed
- Revenue from Deals
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
- Lead to Deal Conversion %
- Average Cash Conversion Cycle

## ROAS Goals by Market

| Market Type | Goal ROAS | Cash Conversion Cycle Goal |
|---|---|---|
| 900K ARV+ Market | 3x | < 90 Days |
| 850K – 400K ARV Market | 5x | < 90 Days |
| < 300K ARV Market | 7x | < 90 Days |

## Notes
- Tracker was empty at time of retrieval (no campaigns entered yet)
- ROAS targets vary by market tier — higher ARV markets require lower ROAS because deal size is larger
- Cash Conversion Cycle: time from lead acquisition to cash received
