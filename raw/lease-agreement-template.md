# Lease Agreement Template

**Description:** Standard lease agreement template used across the Webber portfolio.
**File type:** PDF

## Key Provisions Extracted

**Late Fee:** $40/day after the 5th day rent is due

**First Month's Rent:** Due upon start of Lease Term

**Security Deposit:** Tenant not obligated to pay Security Deposit (per this version — "Judy Esther Lease Agreement")

**Possession:** Tenant accepts premises in current condition

**Access:** Landlord provides keys/fobs as needed; duplicates require landlord consent; all returned at end of lease

**Subletting:** Tenant cannot sublet without prior written consent of Landlord

**Abandonment:** If vacated 7 days or minimum state law, Landlord may terminate immediately

**Right of Entry:** During normal working hours with proper notice for inspection/repairs

**Utilities:** Tenant responsible for all utilities

**Maintenance:** Tenant maintains premises in clean/sanitary manner at own expense; Landlord responsible for interior/exterior repairs; no battery guarantee on appliances

**Early Termination:** Not allowed except domestic violence victims per applicable law

**Pets:** One pet allowed (cats or dogs); no pet fee; tenant responsible for all pet damage; no weight limit

**Noise/Waste:** No nuisance; comply with local noise ordinances

**Guests:** No other persons living on premises; guests limited to 60 days unless approved

**Smoking:** Permitted anywhere on premises (per this template version)

**Default:** Landlord may terminate for non-payment or failure to comply with Agreement provisions

**Governing Law:** State of Texas

## Move-In Checklist Areas
- Living Room (floors, walls, ceiling, windows, lighting, outlets)
- Dining Room
- Kitchen (stove/oven, refrigerator, sink, floors, cabinets, exhaust fan, fire alarms)
- Bedrooms (doors/locks, closets, floors, walls)
- Bathrooms (sink, shower/tub, toilet, doors/locks)
- Other (heating, AC, hot water heater, smoke alarms)

## Lead-Based Paint Disclosure
- Pre-1978 construction disclosure required
- Two options: known hazards present, or no known hazards

## Recurring ACH Payment Authorization
- Authorizes recurring ACH debit on 5th of each month
- NSF fee: $50 per returned item
