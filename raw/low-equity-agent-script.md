## Initial Call (CALL 1)

**You**: Hey (First Name) — Pause and wait for them to confirm

**You**: This is (Your Name) — Pause and wait for them to acknowledge

I was calling you about your listing on (Address). Is this property still available?

**NO**: Ok well no worries, are the sellers currently under contract?

(Confirms that they are under contract) I would like for us to be a backup offer if anything was to fall through. Around when could I follow up with you?

**YES**: So I was looking on the market and noticed that the seller's mortgage balance is (insert number) and the listing price is (insert number). I'm assuming that the sellers might not have a lot of wiggle room on price, is that right?

(Wait for them to respond)

**What do you think the sellers' thoughts would be if I was able to write them a check of their equity, pay your commission, and handle their closing cost?**

**Wait Till They Confirm**

**Pitch**: So my company can do all of this as well as remove this home from their debt to income ratio so they can finance another property. This is through a process called sub to. I believe this could be beneficial for you and your client to know more about.

**HOOK**: Josh Webber, who is the owner of my company, has a ton of experience and can explain more in detail how this works. If from that call you believe that this could be an opportunity, you and him can schedule a meeting with the seller. I see that Josh has some availability this evening or tomorrow afternoon for a call. Which would work best for you?

(They confirm that they are open to hearing more)

Give them the next available day and times. Make sure they understand that this is in Central Standard Time.

And just for scheduling purposes what was a good email for you?

**(IF BOOKED FOR MORE THAN A DAY OUT ONLY)**

So I'll schedule your call with Josh for (Date AND Time) and you'll receive a text to confirm this time. You'll need to reply back with one word "YES" that is spelled Y-E-S. This trigger response is essential to secure your slot. If we don't receive it, the call will be automatically canceled. Would you mind looking out for that text?

Perfect, well I have everything completed on my end. I'll make sure Josh knows what time to call and I do appreciate you for your time (Agents Name). I hope you have a great rest of your day.

## Rebuttals

1. **We have already received offers like this and my client has said no.**
   - Answer: (Pause to act like you are thinking) Hmm, What was your seller's biggest concern for a deal like this?

2. **Are you looking to pay cash or are you guys looking to take over payments?**
   - Answer: So I'm assuming that you guys have already received a lot of creative offers? Since you guys are not under contract yet what rebuttal did the sellers have?

3. **My clients are just looking to do something more conventional.**
   - Answer: I understand that your clients would like to receive their equity and walk away. What we show is how they can do exactly that through this process.

4. **The sellers just don't want the mortgage in their name?**
   - Answer: I totally understand that and a lot of agents tell us the same thing. However what many sellers and agents are not aware of are the protections in place and that it is still possible for them to finance another home.

5. **If they say that the seller has not given an explanation or if you have already covered another rebuttal:**
   - Have you by chance ever closed on a Subto transaction like this?
   - No: Did you happen to have any doubts or concerns on this type of transaction for your client?
   - Yes: From your experience do you believe that an offer like this could help the seller or that there's more risk for them in the long run?

6. **The sellers are going through a Short Sale:**
   - Oh ok, So what I understand is that the sellers are in a situation where they may be upside down owing more on the home than it's worth. Believe it or not we still can be able to purchase a home that has negative equity with a process that accomplishes the same results as a short sale.

7. **Ultimately they are persisted on the sellers not being interested and Do Not Want to Schedule a call:**
   - Answer: I totally understand that my offer is not traditional or might not be preferred. However if a month or so goes by I think it could be helpful for us to schedule a conversation with Josh at that point, just so you're fully informed. Is that ok?

8. **This property is currently under contract:**
   - Answer: Ok I understand, do you have a date that the home is set to close by. I can probably follow up if anything falls through.

9. **Who do you work for?**
   - I work with a team that purchases properties across Texas and I'm the outreach associate. I was calling to see whether this home was still available.
