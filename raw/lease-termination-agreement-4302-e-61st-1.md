LEASE TERMINATION AGREEMENT
4302 East 61st Street, Lubbock, Texas
PARTIES Landlord: W&M Series LLC - E 61st Street Series (the Landlord). Tenant: Jalissa Ramos Mendoza (the Tenant). Property: 4302 East 61st Street, Lubbock, Texas.
AGREEMENT This Lease Termination Agreement is entered into between the parties identified above with respect to the Tenant's current lease at the Property. The parties agree to the following terms governing the termination of that lease and any options for reinstatement.
1. Termination Fee In connection with the termination of Tenant's lease, a termination fee of $3,000.00 is due and payable to Landlord. This amount represents Tenant's obligation upon vacating the Property under the terms of this Agreement.
2. Option to Reinstate — Written Notice Required Before June 30 Tenant has the option to reinstate her lease by providing written notice to Landlord before June 30. Because Tenant's monthly rent payment is due on the 25th of each month, the following applies depending on when written notice is received: a) Notice received before June 25: Landlord will reinstate Tenant's lease. The full $3,000.00 termination fee will be credited toward Tenant's future lease payments or applied toward the balance of any seller finance agreement between the parties, at Tenant's election. b) Notice received on or after June 25 but before June 30: Tenant's June 25 payment will first be deducted from the $3,000.00 termination fee held by Landlord. The remaining credit balance will then be applied toward Tenant's future lease payments or toward the balance of any seller finance agreement, at Tenant's election. Landlord will reinstate Tenant's lease upon receipt of such notice. c) Written notice of reinstatement must be received in writing. Verbal notice will not be accepted.
3. Marketing and Reinstatement Window After June 30 If Tenant does not provide written notice of reinstatement before June 30, Landlord reserves the right to begin actively marketing the Property to prospective tenants or buyers. Notwithstanding such marketing activity, Tenant retains the right to reinstate her lease up until the time Landlord receives a second deposit from a new prospective tenant or buyer. Upon receipt of that deposit, Tenant's option to reinstate is permanently waived and this Agreement shall govern the full termination of Tenant's interest in the Property.
4. Credit Applied After June 25 Payment

If Tenant provides written notice of reinstatement after the June 25 payment has been processed, that payment amount will be deducted from the $3,000.00 termination fee. Any remaining credit balance may, at Tenant's election, be applied toward:
a) Any future scheduled payment;
b) Any past-due balance owed under the lease; or
c) Tenant's seller finance agreement terms.
5. Entire Agreement This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties with respect to the termination of the lease at the Property and supersedes all prior discussions or representations. Any modification must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
