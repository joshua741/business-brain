# THE Promissory Note & Deed of Trust — Template

**Description:** Master promissory note and deed of trust template for creative finance deals (Private Lender Contractor Pack — Funding Kit).

## Deed of Trust (Template)

**Structure:**
- Grantor (property owner/borrower)
- Grantee (lender)
- Property description and legal description required

**Key Terms to Fill:**
- Principal amount ($###.##)
- Interest rate (# per cent per annum)
- Monthly payment amount
- Payment start date and maturity date
- Payment address

**Key Provisions:**
- Prepayment: allowed without penalty with advance written notice
- Due-on-sale clause: Grantee may declare full balance due if property sold/transferred without consent (exceptions: subordinate liens, purchase money security for appliances, devise/descent, leases ≤3 years without option to purchase)
- Insurance: must maintain coverage; Grantee named as loss payee
- Tax payment: Grantor must pay all taxes/assessments when due
- Default: triggers acceleration of full balance; 30-day written notice required before foreclosure advertising
- Power of sale: Granted to Grantee for auction at courthouse; 4-week newspaper advertising; Grantee may bid at sale
- Rent assignment: Grantor assigns all future rents to Grantee (dormant unless default)
- Subrogation: Grantee subrogated to claims/liens discharged with loan proceeds

## Promissory Note (Template)

**Key Terms:**
- Principal amount
- Interest rate (charged on unpaid principal)
- Monthly payment on specific day of month
- Balloon/maturity date
- Right to prepay without penalty with advance notice

**Default Provisions:**
- Late charge: ___% for payments 15+ days overdue
- Default: Grantee serves 30-day written notice of default, then may accelerate full balance
- Borrower pays all costs and attorney fees for enforcement

**Important:** This is a Private Lender Contractor Pack template — used for structuring loans from private money lenders (PMLs) or transactional lenders (TLs) for creative finance deals.
