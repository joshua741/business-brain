## How to Use

### Step 1 — Labor Cost
Enter average market pay rate and a Boost % (extra for raises, lead pay, bonuses). Calculator computes true all-in hourly cost including all payroll taxes. Texas rates pre-loaded.

### Step 2 — Cleaning Time
Enter total sq ft and carpet vs. hard floor split. Add fixture/area counts (toilets, sinks, desks, clinical rooms, break areas, lobbies, windows). Calculator estimates total hours using standard cleaning speeds.

### Step 3 — Final Details
Enter cleaning frequency per week, supply cost (target 2–3% of monthly price), and sales tax rate if applicable.
