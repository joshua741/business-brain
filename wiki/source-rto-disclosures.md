# Source: RTO & Seller-Finance Disclosures and FAQs

**Summary**: The R2OC-facing program documents — buyer FAQs, tenant financing disclosure, move-in disclosure, seller-finance listing description, and the credit-repair referral channel.

**Sources**: see frontmatter

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

Customer-facing documents for the [[r2oc]] [[rent-to-own]] / [[seller-finance]] programs. Full program terms are captured on those concept pages. Highlights:
- **FAQs** — RTO ($3,500–5,000 option fee, 520+ credit, 2.5x income) vs seller finance (600+ credit, 3x income, 5% prepay penalty); full comparison table (source: faq-rent-to-own.md, rent-to-own-and-seller-finance-faq.md).
- **Tenant Financing Disclosure** (Google Doc + fillable PDF) — on-time-payment eligibility ladder for converting renters to buyers (source: tenant-financing-disclosure.md, fillable-the-tenant-financing-disclosure.md).
- **R2O Move-In Disclosure** — Lubbock utility contacts, owner-mindset framing, late/eviction ladder (3-day grace, $50/day, Notice to Quit at 7 days, eviction at 14) (source: u-r2o-move-in-disclosure.md).
- **Seller Financing Description** — 126 Paul St, San Antonio example ($285k, 10% down, 7%, ~$2,175.62/mo) (source: seller-financing-description.md).
- **Credit-Repair Referral** — down-payment/interest scale (10% down=8%, …, 5% down=13%); first month's payment as referral fee (source: credit-repair-email-seller-finance.md).
- **Down Payment Calculator** — quick price→payment mapping (source: down-payment-calculator-seller-finance-and-rto.md).
