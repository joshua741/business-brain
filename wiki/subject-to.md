# Subject-To

**Summary**: Acquiring a property by taking title subject to the existing mortgage. Seller stays on the loan; buyer makes payments.

**Sources**: CLAUDE.md seed

**Last updated**: 2026-05-28

---

A creative acquisition strategy where Joshua takes title to a property while the existing mortgage remains in the seller's name. Joshua makes the mortgage payments. Allows acquisition with little or no cash down. Primary reference: [[pace-morby]]. Part of the [[creative-financing]] toolkit.
