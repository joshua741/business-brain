# Subject-To

**Summary**: Acquiring a property by taking title subject to the existing mortgage. Seller stays on the loan; buyer makes payments.

**Sources**: CLAUDE.md seed

**Last updated**: 2026-05-28

---

A creative acquisition strategy where Joshua takes title to a property while the existing mortgage remains in the seller's name. Joshua makes the mortgage payments. Allows acquisition with little or no cash down. Primary reference: [[pace-morby]]. Part of the [[creative-financing]] toolkit.

## In practice
- Sourced via the [[low-equity-agent-script]] (within [[sales-scripts]]) — pitching agents on paying the seller's equity, covering commission/closing, and removing the mortgage from the seller's DTI.
- The [[2802-s-channing-st]] hold (Mr. Cooper-serviced underlying mortgage) and several portfolio properties (4626 S Lipscomb, 4438 Parker, 3904 Ave R, 4618 45th, 7005 Winston per the n8n list) are subject-to / sub-to+seller-finance structures.
- Modeled with PML layering on [[4302-e-61st-st]]. For sellers in/near bankruptcy, the limited POA template (see [[legal-document-templates]]) lets WIH manage lender comms.
