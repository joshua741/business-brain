# Agent Outreach

**Summary**: Direct-to-AGENT acquisition outreach — the dominant modern wholesale channel and the Phase-1 engine of the [[no-fluff-model]], synthesized from [[matt-beard]], [[hieu]], and [[alex-martinez]].

**Sources**: clip-2026-06-03-ultimate-agent-outreach-script-for-wholesalers-2025.md, clip-2026-06-03-hieu-1-3m-process-FULL.md, clip-2026-06-03-agent-script-freescript-FULL.md

**Last updated**: 2026-06-03

---

Direct-to-agent outreach has overtaken direct-to-seller as the dominant acquisition channel in modern wholesale. The thesis: listing agents are gatekeepers to motivated sellers, and you can incentivize them to bring your offer to the seller.

## Core principles
Synthesized from [[matt-beard]], [[hieu]], and [[alex-martinez]]:
- **Never say "investor"** — say you're "looking to buy a home/property."
- **Incentivize the listing agent** via dual commission / the "double dip" — offer up to 6–10% so the agent pitches your offer and tells the seller it's net to them.
- **Always end every objection answer with a question** to keep control of the conversation.
- **Get them on a PHONE CALL** — texts exist only to book the call.
- **Volume**: 300–500 sends/day per market.
- **Off-market focus cuts cost-per-deal ~4x** (12–20 leads/deal vs. 41–62 on-market).
- **Cadence example** ([[hieu]]): 5 texts → wait 60 days → 5 more, auto-remove on reply.
- **Target agents 45–60 minutes outside the metro.**

This is the Phase-1 engine of the [[no-fluff-model]] and the functional spec for [[vince-ai]] + [[ghl]] texting automation. The full objection bank and scripts live in [[sales-scripts]].
