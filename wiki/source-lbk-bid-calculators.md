# Source: LBK Cleaners Bid & Quote Calculators

**Summary**: The commercial-cleaning pricing tools for [[lbk-cleaners]] — true-labor-cost + cleaning-time + margin calculators, pre-loaded with Lubbock/Texas rates, plus a Lubbock market-research template.

**Sources**: lbk-cleaners-bid-calculator-1.md; quote-calculator.md; quote-calculator-improved.md; lubbock-market-research-template.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

Three iterations of the bid tool (Bid Calculator, Quote Calculator, Quote Calculator Improved) compute: true hourly labor cost (base pay × (1 + payroll-liability %), ~$17.70 all-in at $14 base + 14.95%), cleaning time by sqft/production-rate + fixture counts, and monthly price by target margin. Suggested margin scales by weekly hours (60% at 0–3 hrs down to 35% at 66+); commercial target ~45%. Includes production-rate reference by facility type, apartment-complex add-ons, a Quick Eyeball Bid, and a bid-history log (source: lbk-cleaners-bid-calculator-1.md, quote-calculator-improved.md, quote-calculator.md).

**Lubbock market context:** median janitor wage $12.95/hr (BLS 2024, ~25% below national); office $0.05–0.25/sqft/visit; apartment unit turns $45–120 (source: lbk-cleaners-bid-calculator-1.md). The Lubbock market-research template also holds Airbnb/STR comp data (source: lubbock-market-research-template.md).
