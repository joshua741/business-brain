# Google Sheets

**Summary**: KPI tracking, financial dashboards, content automation for R2OC, and BookingKoala booking event sync.

**Sources**: CLAUDE.md seed, BookingKoala_Help_Center_Full_Reference.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

Four primary uses:

1. **Daily KPI tracking** — Joshua reviews each morning.
2. **Profit First financial tracking** — alongside [[mercury]].
3. **Content AI automation** — drives daily social media posts for [[r2oc]] via [[content-ai]].
4. **BookingKoala sync** — [[bookingkoala]] has a native Google Sheets integration that syncs booking events (new bookings, cancellations, charges, provider payouts) to a spreadsheet. Useful for [[lbk-cleaners]] reporting and visibility into booking volume, revenue, and cleaner activity without logging into the platform.
