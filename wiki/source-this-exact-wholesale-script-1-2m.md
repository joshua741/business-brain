# This EXACT Wholesale Real Estate Script Made $1.2M In 12 Months

**Summary**: Alex Martinez's (RealEstateSkills.com) MLS distressed-property two-call closing framework — a 15-part Discovery Call (gather info, no offer) followed by a Close Call, plus an objection-as-deal-multiplier rebuttal system.

The [[mls-two-call-closing]] framework from [[alex-martinez]]: an on-market MLS acquisition channel that complements [[matt-beard]]'s off-market [[no-fluff-model]].

## Core method / framework

### Call 1 — Discovery (gather info, NO offer), 15 parts (verbatim)
1. **Warm intro** — "Hey [Agent], this is Alex with ABC Houses... I wanted to get more info about your listing at 123 Main Street. Are you open to chatting?" (if busy → B.A.M.F.A.M. = Book A Meeting From A Meeting)
2. **Representation** (key lever) — "First off, I'm a buyer and not an agent. So would you be open to representing me as your buyer?" (dual commission ~$12K); if they can't, ask for a referral (~25% / ~$1,500); ninja tip: offer to waive buyer commission.
3. "what's the seller's reason for selling?"
4. **Condition** (via photos) — "what would you say is the overall condition?"
5. "Are there any major issues... structural, foundational, or electrical?"
6. "Has anything been upgraded recently?"
7. "anything particularly special about this property?"
8. "What would you say this home could be worth in its best possible condition once renovated?"
9. **Activity** — "How's the current activity?... a lot of calls or offers?... homeowner or investor?... How many?... any other offers coming?"
10. "What price do I have to come in at today to get it under contract?" (BE SILENT) → "And how flexible is that price?" → "Is the seller willing to review offers less than that?"
11. "Are there any specific offer terms your seller would like?"
12. "When does the seller plan on reviewing and deciding on offers?"
13. "Are you currently representing any other buyers?"
14. **Deal extraction** — "Do you know of any other fixer-type properties... not even on the market... We're looking to buy five more deals this month, and I'd love to have you represent us on each one."
15. **Wrap** — "We're in this business to build long-term relationships... justified with thorough research... I'll get back to you as soon as possible."

### Call 2 — Close
Re-establish rapport, then: "After accounting for everything... we can get an all-cash offer in today with a 7-day inspection, a 14-day close, and we can offer $350,000." (BE SILENT.) Confirm email, DocuSign vs. wet signature, "What time will you send the contract back?"

### Deal multipliers (objection handling — "celebrate objections")
- **Higher investor offers** → seed doubt: "Do you have 100% confidence in this company with the higher offer? inexperienced investors offer too high then cancel..."
- **Too low** → educate with the 75%-sell-below-list stat + cost stack
- **Highest and best** → "the highest offer isn't the best offer... I can definitely be the best offer"
- **Backup** → "How about we just be the backup offer?"

## Numbers & benchmarks
- $1.2M in 12 months
- 1 in 15 offers → a $10K+ fee
- 75% of houses sell below list
- ~50% of higher competitor offers fall out
- Cost stack: $60K repairs / $7K taxes / $12K commissions / $9K holding
- Dual commission: $6K → $12K
