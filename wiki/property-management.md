# Property Management

**Summary**: Self-managed property operations across the WIH portfolio, with Mostafa as operational lead and AI automation handling routine workflows.

**Sources**: CLAUDE.md seed

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

[[wih]] manages its own portfolio rather than using a third-party property manager. [[mostafa]] is the operational lead — handling tenant comms, maintenance coordination, and day-to-day issues. [[doorloop]] is the platform for leases, charges, and maintenance tracking.

Strategic direction: full AI automation of property management workflows. [[ghl]] is the hub for all tenant-facing communications. [[ai-automation-strategy]] covers the broader roadmap across all three verticals including this one.
