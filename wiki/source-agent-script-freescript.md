## Core method / framework

The full DISCOVERY CALL script for calling listing agents on distressed MLS leads. Preserve verbatim and in order:

**1. Warm intro:**
> "Hey Louis, this is Alex with ABC Houses. How are you doing today?... I wanted to give you a call about your listing on Laurie St. Did I catch you at a bad time? Are you able to chat about it right now?"

**2. Representation:**
> "first off, I am a buyer, not an agent. So would you be open to representing me as your buyer by any chance?"

(If no → get the referral agent's name / phone / email.)

**3. Reason for selling:**
> "what's the seller's reason for selling?"

**4. Condition:**
> "What would you say is the overall condition of the property?"

**5. Major issues:**
> "Are there any major issues... structural? Foundational? Any faulty wiring or electrical?"

**6 / 7. Upgrades & special features:**
> "Is the kitchen updated? Are the bathrooms updated? Any special features...?"

**8. Agent's ARV:**
> "What would you say this home would be worth in its best possible condition, once renovated?"

**9. Activity:**
> "How's the current activity been? A lot of calls or offers? Homeowner or investor offers? How many? Any more coming in?"

**10. Price to lock:**
> "What price do I need to come in at today to actually lock it up today?"

then

> "How flexible is that price?"

**11. Offer terms:**
> "Are there any specific offer terms the seller would like to see?"

(Standard: close 14 days, 7-day inspection.)

**12. Review timeline:**
> "When does the seller plan on reviewing offers?"

**13. Other representation:**
> "are you representing anyone else on this property?"

**14. Deal extraction (pipeline multiplier):**
> "Do you know of any other fixer-type properties... not even on the market yet that you can represent me on? We're looking to buy 5 more deals this month and would love to have you represent me on each one."

**15. Wrap:**
> "We're in this business to build long-term relationships... I'm not just going to come in with some wild random offer price... I'll get back to you ASAP."

**Supporting frameworks:**
- **Hierarchy of Representation** — LA represents you (dual commission) → LA refers you (referral fee) → represent yourself.
- **Amateurs vs Pros** — Amateurs text/email, mass-blast, transactional. Pros call, build rapport, treat agents as partners.
- **Win-Win-Win.**
- **Poker analogy:** "the deals are in the data."
- Expect a **2nd follow-up call** — "the deals are in the follow-up."

## Numbers & benchmarks

- Dual commission ~$12K vs ~$6K single side.
- Referral fee ~25% (~$1,500).
- Standard offer terms: 14-day close, 7-day inspection.
