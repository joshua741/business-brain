# Draw Reimbursements

**Summary**: The process of tracking contractor draws and materials reimbursements against rehab budgets (often a hard-money construction holdback), used on W&M Series rehab projects.

**Sources**: draw-reimbursement-sheet-2102-68th-st.md; draw-reimbursement-sheet-1926-27th-st.md; shelby-2102-68th-st-labor-cost-bid-sheet-repair-list.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

For rehab projects, WIH tracks contractor labor draws and materials (Home Depot / Lowe's / Sutherland carts) against a phased labor budget — typically funded by a lender construction holdback (e.g. Kiavi's $38,200 on [[2102-68th-st]]) released in draws. Primary contractor: **Shelby McDonald**; also **33 Carpentry & Construction** (source: draw-reimbursement-sheet-2102-68th-st.md, draw-reimbursement-sheet-1926-27th-st.md).

Standard phasing (per Shelby bid sheets): Site Prep & Demo → Structural & Rough Work → Walls & Surfaces → Finishes & Fixtures → Details & Finishes; ~$18,000 total labor budget per house; ~3-week timeline (source: shelby-2102-68th-st-labor-cost-bid-sheet-repair-list.md). Tracked via Google Sheets and reconciled against [[baselane]] statements.

Active on: [[2102-68th-st]], [[1926-27th-st]].
