# Loan Servicing

**Summary**: Third-party servicing of owner-financed notes (seller finance, subject-to, bond-for-deed) so payment collection and disbursement run without Joshua's direct involvement. Primary servicer: Southern Loan Servicing (SLS).

**Sources**: sls-servicing-agreement.md; the-promissory-note-deed-of-trust.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

To keep [[seller-finance]] and [[creative-financing]] notes hands-off, WIH uses **Southern Loan Servicing (SLS)** — a Louisiana-licensed bond-for-deed escrow agent (Harvey, LA). SLS collects buyer payments, disburses to the underlying mortgage / seller, issues statements, handles late notices, payoff demands, and tax forms (1098-INT to lenders, 1099-INT to borrowers) (source: sls-servicing-agreement.md).

## Fees (Appendix A)
$50/mo servicing fee; setup $275–$450 by tier; NSF/stop-pay $35; payoff quote $50; tax mgmt $45/yr; insurance mgmt $45/check; reinstatement $375; misc $125/hr (source: sls-servicing-agreement.md).

## Payment waterfall
SLS fees → underlying mortgage P&I + escrow → seller P&I → other charges. ACH must fund by the 20th of the preceding month; no re-deposit of returned items. SLS is not a collection agency or property manager (source: sls-servicing-agreement.md).

Referenced as the servicer for the R2OC seller-finance program (third-party serviced) and applicable to notes like the [[1312-65th-dr]] Dillon Ford deed of trust.
