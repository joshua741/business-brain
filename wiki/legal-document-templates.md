# Legal Document Templates

**Summary**: The reusable contract, trust, lease, and disclosure templates used across [[wih]] acquisitions, dispositions, and tenant placements — most Texas-governed, supporting [[wholesale]], [[creative-financing]], and [[property-management]].

**Sources**: assignment-of-contract templates; purchase agreements; TREC amendment/exhibit forms; trust & POA templates; lease templates; tenant disclosures

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

## Acquisition / disposition contracts
- **Assignment of Contract** — two branded versions: WIH (Webber Investment Homes LLC) and WWH (Webber Wealth Holdings LLC) as seller/assignor. AS-IS, cash-sale addendum, 90-day title-cure window, time-of-the-essence, $200/day-or-0.1% extension penalty, non-assignable by buyer (source: wih-assignment-of-contract.md, wwh-assignment-of-contract.md).
- **Fillable Double-Close Purchase Agreement** — buyer "Webber Investment Homes LLC and/or assigns"; supports cash, third-party financing, loan assumption, seller finance, and [[subject-to]]; option-fee termination right (source: fillable-double-close-purchase-agreement.md).
- **Fillable Texas Residential Real Estate Purchase Agreement** — 8-page standard with cash/bank/seller-finance options and mediation→arbitration→court dispute ladder (source: fillable-texas-residential-real-estate-purchase-agreement.md).
- **TREC Amendment to Contract (No. 39-8)** and **Exhibit A** — standard Texas amendment to change price, closing date, repairs, option terms (source: amendment-to-contract.md, exhibit-a.md). Used e.g. on [[4513-48th-st]].

## Notes & trusts
- **Promissory Note & Deed of Trust template** (Private Lender Contractor Pack) — due-on-sale, power of sale, rent assignment, prepayment-without-penalty; used for seller-finance notes and [[pml-tl]] lending (source: the-promissory-note-deed-of-trust.md).
- **Fillable Revocable Living Trust** (legaltemplates.net) — the structure used to hold properties like [[1312-65th-dr]]; spendthrift clause, homestead exemption, successor trustees, Schedules A–D (source: fillable-revocable-living-trust.md).
- **Limited POA for Bankruptcy** (Texas) — lets WIH act as attorney-in-fact for sellers in/near bankruptcy to handle lender/loss-mitigation comms on [[subject-to]]/seller-finance deals; cannot transfer title or file pleadings (source: limited-poa-bankruptcy-fillable.md).

## Leases & tenant disclosures
- **Standard / template / "fix" / filled lease examples** — Texas residential leases; $40/day late fee after 5-day grace, tenant utilities, ACH-on-the-5th authorization ($50 NSF), lead-based-paint disclosure, move-in checklist. Note template variants differ on pets and smoking (source: lease-agreement-template.md, standard-lease-agreement-3.md, lease-agreement-template-fix.md, filled-lease-example.md).
- **Addendum — AS-IS / Tenant Repair Responsibility** — shifts virtually all repairs to tenant (Texas Prop. Code §§92.006/92.052), 100-item damage list, tenant pays insurance deductible, renters insurance $100k+ with landlord as additional insured (source: addendum-to-residential-lease-agreement.md).
- **Addendum — Automatic Bank Draft Requirement** — mandatory ACH enrollment, 5%-of-rent non-compliance fee (source: u-fillable-addendum-to-lease-bank-draft-payment-requirement.md).
- **Tenant Financing Disclosure** (Google Doc + fillable PDF) — discloses potential [[seller-finance]] terms and the on-time-payment eligibility ladder (months 0–36) used to convert renters to buyers (source: tenant-financing-disclosure.md, fillable-the-tenant-financing-disclosure.md).
- **R2O Move-In Disclosure** — Lubbock utility setup (LP&L, TXU, Atmos), AS-IS / owner-mindset framing, 2-month reserve recommendation (promotes Sequence app), and the late-payment/eviction ladder (3-day grace, $50/day to 12% cap, Notice to Quit at 7 days, eviction at 14) (source: u-r2o-move-in-disclosure.md). Branded [[r2oc]].

> **Note (lint):** Lease template versions conflict on pet/smoking policy (one allows smoking anywhere and unlimited pet weight; the tenant script and 1312 65th Dr enforce smoke-free and 50-lb limits). Confirm the canonical current lease. See [[aaron-mccloskey]] smoking violation at [[1312-65th-dr]].
