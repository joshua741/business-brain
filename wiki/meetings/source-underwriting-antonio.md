## Key takeaways
- Sell price = **105тАУ110% of ARV** (round to a clean promo number, e.g. $225k) (source: clip-2026-06-03-pre-foreclosure-underwriting-with-antonio-and-josh-2026-05-0.md).
- **Interest rate is set by ARV band, NOT credit** тАФ deals are interest-rate driven:
  - ARV $120kтАУ$150k тЖТ **9.5%**
  - ARV $151kтАУ$210k тЖТ **9.0%**
  - ARV $211k+ тЖТ **8.5%**
- Strategy is to **start rates low and raise slowly** as the portfolio grows тАФ "we're not greedy."
- A mortgage = **PITI** (Principal, Interest, Taxes, Insurance); add **PMI** when <20% down. The buyer's escrow payment mirrors the underlying mortgage's.
- **Rent-to-own payment** = compute buyer PITI, then **round up to the highest tenth** (e.g. $1,697.68 тЖТ $1,700).
- **R2O deposit тЙИ 3.5% down**.
- **Don't run numbers before talking to the seller** тАФ avoid "analysis paralysis"; conversations first, math second.
- **Property management fee = 2.5%** of the selling rent.
- **Net-margin goal = 20%** of the selling rent тАФ protects scalability and reinvestment into team/software.

## Framework тАФ cash-flow underwriting (rule-of-thumb SOP)
1. **Sell price** = ARV ├Ч 1.05тАУ1.10 (round to clean number).
2. **Interest rate** by ARV band (9.5% / 9.0% / 8.5% per above).
3. **Buyer escrow (PITI)** mirrors the underlying mortgage; PMI if <20% down.
4. **R2O payment** = buyer PITI rounded up to the highest tenth.
5. **Deposit** = 3.5% down.
6. **Insurance estimate (rule of thumb, /mo)**: $120тАУ150k ARV тЖТ ~$200; $151тАУ210k тЖТ ~$250тАУ300; $211тАУ300k+ тЖТ ~$250тАУ350.
7. **PMI estimate (rule of thumb, /mo)**: $120тАУ150k тЖТ ~$100; $151тАУ210k тЖТ ~$150; $211тАУ300k+ тЖТ ~$175тАУ200.
8. **Net margin**: Selling Rent тИТ PM fee (2.5%) = Net Gross Rent; Net Gross Rent тИТ actual mortgage PITI = True Net. **Target True Net тЙИ 20% of selling rent.**

*Worked example in the video:* ARV $205k тЖТ sell ~$225k; 9% / 30-yr; 3.5% down ($7,875) тЖТ P&I тЙИ $1,747; taxes $3,500/yr ├╖ 12; insurance ~$225/mo; PMI ~$150/mo.
