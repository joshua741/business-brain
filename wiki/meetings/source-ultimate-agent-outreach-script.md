# Ultimate Agent Outreach Script for Wholesalers (2025) — Matt Beard (No Fluff)

## Core method / framework

Acquisition-side cold-call / text script to get pocket / off-market listings from agents. Preserve verbatim.

**Opener (assume prior contact):**
- Call: "Hi [First Name], it's [full name], how are you?... I reached out around a month ago and we hadn't gotten a chance to connect. It was about properties that needed some work."
- Text: "Hey, just checking back in, see if anything's coming up that needs some work. This is [Name] by the way."

**Don't remember / nothing:**
> "Great, I was just following up... I wanted to see if you had anything coming up that hasn't hit the market yet?"

**If nothing:**
> "Gotcha, well I'll take a look at just about anything. How often do you run across properties that need some work or aren't market ready?"

**Relationship pivot:**
> "we buy all kinds of properties including multifamily and commercial. Honestly, I try to set myself apart... by putting a voice to the name. Do you know of anyone that may have something coming soon? We'd be happy to have you represent us."

**Tier-3 follow-up OK:**
> "Is it okay if I follow up in a month or so? ... Look for a text from [number] so you can save it!"

**If they have something:**
> "Perfect, are you in the position to share any details yet?" → address → "What can you tell me about the property that I can't find online?" → "What are they looking to get for it?" → "I will be getting this over to the underwriting team, and we will be giving you a call within the next hour or so."

**OBJECTION BANK** (rule: finish every objection answer with a question back):
- **POF:** "Absolutely I can email you our proof of funds and our buy box..."
- **Buy box:** "Typically we buy in and around the median price point... at least 3 beds and 2 baths and newer than 1970. Ideally needs some work."
- **Investor / wholesaler:** "We are end buyers that primarily focus on Fix and Flip... also wholesale out of state..."
- **Company:** "we are a pretty small operation... pay a little more because we don't have a big overhead."
- **How'd you get my number:** "I saw you had a property off [street] that looked amazing..."
- **Agent paid:** "we either have the agent represent us or we figure out a finders fee..."
- **Brokerage agreement:** "we'll be happy to sign anything that states... you get paid a commission or fee!"
- **Don't know you:** "Shoot! I'm sorry this is [name]... I reached out about fixers about a month ago."
- **Remove me:** "no problem... But before you go, we bought a property recently that one of our agent partners had a tough time selling... Do you ever represent buyers?"

> "Finish every objection answer with a question back to the prospect!!"

## Numbers & benchmarks

- Off-market: 12–20 leads/deal vs on-market 41–62 (~4x better).
- Avg deal ~$18K.
- Volume: 300–500 sends/day → 3–8 properties/day.
- Cost $100–150/deal.
- Margin 75–78%.

> "Have you reached out to four to 500 of them a day for a month straight?"
