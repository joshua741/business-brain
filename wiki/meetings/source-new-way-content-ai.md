# The New Way of Making Content In The Age of AI

**Summary**: Alex Hormozi's thesis that AI disrupts low-risk creators first — so proof and credibility win — and that the way to build a moat is to "do epic shit and document it" via a self-licking ice cream cone.

**Sources**: clip-2026-06-03-the-new-way-of-making-content-in-the-age-of-ai.md

**Last updated**: 2026-06-03

---

[[alex-hormozi]] (Acquisition.com), one of Joshua's role models, on content in the AI age.

## Core method / framework
**Low-Risk → High-Risk creator continuum** (AI disrupts low-risk first):
1. **Entertainers** — most disruptable
2. **B2C Educators** — low-stakes behavior
3. **B2 Prosumer** — money/investing; needs real credibility (e.g. Erika Kullberg, Vivian Tu, Dave Ramsey)
4. **B2B Creators** — business advice; hardest for AI to fake

Thesis: as consumer risk rises, required proof/demonstration rises — "the person who has the most credibility wins, by a lot."

- **"Do epic shit and document it"** — capture real results, don't fabricate; transcribe meetings then "Hey AI, tell me the interesting moments."
- **"Self-licking ice cream cone"** — engineer proof INTO your business motion so delivery and content generation are the same act.
- If you lack proof, do free audits/services publicly and document them.

## Tools
AI avatars, meeting transcription + "interesting moments" prompt, Skool.
