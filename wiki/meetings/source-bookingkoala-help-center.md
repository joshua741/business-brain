## Key Capabilities Extracted

### Booking Forms
Five form types for different pricing structures. For cleaning, the typical choice is:
- **Form 1** — flat rate by pricing parameters (bedrooms/bathrooms)
- **Form 2** — items/packages (specific service bundles)

### Smart Scheduling
Automated scheduling engine that optimizes cleaner assignment based on availability, location, and preferences. Critical to set up before going live.

### Provider (Cleaner) Management
- Full schedule management per cleaner
- Provider app (mobile) for clock-in/out, job notes, customer signatures
- Payment processing via Stripe Connect — cleaners connect their own bank accounts for direct deposit
- IC vs W2 classification per provider
- Import tool for bulk cleaner onboarding

### Hiring Module (23 articles)
Dedicated hiring pipeline inside BookingKoala for recruiting, vetting, and onboarding cleaners. Available on Growing and Premium plans. Key to the [[lbk-cleaners-launch]] next phase.

### Integrations Available
- **Zapier** — 16 triggers (new booking, cancellation, charge, new customer, new provider, payout, etc.). Can connect [[bookingkoala]] events directly to [[ghl]] pipeline via Zapier.
- **Google Calendar** — sync all bookings to Google Calendar; providers get their own calendar view
- **Google Sheets** — sync booking events to Sheets for reporting/tracking
- **QuickBooks** — sync charges and refunds for accounting
- **Stripe/Square/PayPal/Authorize.net** — payment processors; **Stripe Connect** for provider payouts
- **MailChimp** — email lists: abandoned cart, one-time users, recurring users

### Notifications
Email and SMS for: booking confirmations, reminders, completions, cancellations, charges, provider communications. All customizable templates.

### Checklists
Create job checklists that providers complete during the service. Customers can also review post-job.

### Marketing
- Coupons with usage limits, expiry, and pre-pay requirements
- Referral sharing for customers (Growing+)
- Campaign broadcasts (Premium)
- Gift cards (Growing+)

## Setup Sequence for New Business (from Help Center)
1. Store Options (Settings > General > Store Options)
2. Set up booking forms and industries
3. Configure smart scheduling
4. Set up notifications
5. Create and onboard providers/cleaners
6. Configure website/booking page
7. Enable payment processor
8. Activate extra features (Zapier, checklists, hiring module)
