# Source: 53 Alabama Properties

**Summary**: Off-market wholesale pipeline of 53 distressed Alabama properties (Bessemer / Fairfield / Birmingham metro), $12k–$35k listing, mostly not yet sent to buyers.

**Sources**: 53-al-properties.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

A [[wholesale]] sourcing review of 53 mostly off-market AL properties — listing prices $12,000–$35,000, under-contract $5,000–$28,500, ARV $30,000–$210,000 where available. Most "Not Sent"; 3 pushed to Investor Lift; one MLS listing flagged "Issues." Low price points indicate distressed/damaged inventory in the Birmingham metro (source: 53-al-properties.md). Out-of-core-market (Lubbock) sourcing experiment.
