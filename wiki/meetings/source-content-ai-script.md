# Source: Content AI Google Apps Script (May 16, 2026)

**Summary**: Snapshot of the Google Apps Script powering the [[content-ai]] in-sheet approval/posting workflow for [[r2oc]].

**Sources**: paste-may-16-2026-8-48am.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

Google Apps Script for the Rent2OwnCribs content system. Adds a "Content Studio" custom menu (onOpen) and an onEdit audit logger. Functions: approve/deny/mark-posted/request-revision on the Daily Queue, knowledge-base + audience-question management, scheduling, full automation run, and a weekly summary email — all writing to an Audit Log. Expected tabs: Daily Queue, Knowledge Base, Audience Questions, Brand Intelligence, Pre-Schedule Builder, References, Source URLs, Daily/Monthly Content Identity, Dashboard, Approved Library, Audit Log (source: paste-may-16-2026-8-48am.md).
