# Source: Commercial vs. Residential vs. Hybrid Cleaning Business Analysis

**Summary**: Manus AI strategic analysis of all three cleaning models across 15 dimensions. Clear winner: Commercial B2B.

**Sources**: Commercial vs. Residential vs. Hybrid Cleaning Businesses A Comprehensive Strategic Analysis.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

Prepared by Manus AI, May 29, 2026. US cleaning industry: 108.3B, 3.0% CAGR. Grades all three models A-F across 15 dimensions.

## Overall Scores (4.0 GPA Scale)
- **Commercial Cleaning**: 2.67 (B-) — winner
- **Residential Cleaning**: 2.27 (C+) — viable start
- **Hybrid Model**: 1.93 (C) — avoid

## Key Findings by Dimension

**Commercial wins on**: income potential (250K-1M+ annually), scalability (step-wise via large contracts), client retention (12-36 month B2B contracts, avg CLV 14,400-100K+), revenue predictability, competition protection via high barrier to entry, exit value (3.0x-5.5x EBITDA).

**Residential wins on**: startup cost (2K-5K), marketing ease (online booking, 100-300 CAC), work-life balance (M-F 8-5), equipment cost (under 1K/crew), insurance cost (44/mo), immediate same-day cash flow.

**Hybrid fails on**: staffing (two separate labor forces, different hours), marketing (two brands, double CAC), 24/7 owner-on-call, rapid burnout, and operational friction. Rated F on staffing, marketing difficulty, and work-life balance.

## Critical Data Points
- Commercial net margin: 10-28% (specialty niche up to 40%)
- Residential annual churn: 20-30%
- Commercial client CLV: 14,400 (small office) to 100K+ (large facility)
- Commercial labor turnover: 200-400% annually
- Residential labor turnover: 75% annually
- Commercial insurance: 2M+ GL required by corporate clients
- Residential exit value: 1.5x-2.5x SDE
- Commercial exit value: 3.0x-5.5x EBITDA — PE rollup targets

## Strategic Recommendation
Start residential for immediate cash flow and low startup cost. Use residential revenue to fund commercial equipment, insurance, and compliance. Transition to commercial B2B as the long-term wealth-building asset. Full strategy in [[cleaning-business-models]].
