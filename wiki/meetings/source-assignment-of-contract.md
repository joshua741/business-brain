# Source: Assignment of Contract Templates (WIH / WWH)

**Summary**: Two branded wholesale assignment-of-contract templates — Webber Investment Homes LLC and Webber Wealth Holdings LLC as seller/assignor. Identical body text.

**Sources**: wih-assignment-of-contract.md; wwh-assignment-of-contract.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

Standard [[wholesale]] assignment contracts: AS-IS / where-is, cash-sale addendum (proof of funds for hard-money/IRA), 90-day seller title-cure window, time-of-the-essence with earnest money as liquidated damages, buyer extension penalty ($200/day or 0.1% of price), buyer may not re-market without consent, lis-pendens waiver. Marketing/commission line paid from seller proceeds at closing.

The only difference between the two files is the seller entity name (WIH vs WWH). Content detail lives in [[legal-document-templates]].
