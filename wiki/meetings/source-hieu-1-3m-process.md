# Hieu's Exact Process That Generated $1.3M Wholesaling — Jerry Norton interview

**Summary**: Jerry Norton (Flipping Mastery TV) interviews Hieu, a direct-to-AGENT off-market wholesaler running entirely on GoHighLevel — the highest-leverage GHL-native blueprint in the library, covering the full texting sequence, agent call script, follow-up drip, and discovery call.

## Core method / framework

Direct-to-agent off-market wholesaling, fully automated on GoHighLevel. Goal of every touch = get the agent on a phone call. Never cold-call cold, never pure text.

> "The goal is always to get them on a phone call."

**TEXTING SEQUENCE (automated in GHL):**
- Initial: "Hi [Agent], this is Hu. I'm looking to buy a home. Can you call me back and talk about it?"
- If no reply: "Hey, could you help me out or probably not?"
- Or: "Would you be available to give me a call today?"
- Send up to **5 texts → wait 60 days → 5 more**. Open round 2 with: "Are you still active with your license or not?"
- Auto-remove the agent on reply.
- NEVER say "investor" — say "looking to buy a home."

> "you're just saying I'm looking to buy a house."
> "Send out five, wait 60 days, and reach out again five more times."

**AGENT CALL SCRIPT:**
> "Hey [Agent], this is Hu with HBI Realty. How are you doing?... me and my partner are looking to buy another investment property in the [Atlanta] area. Is that something you can help with?... We're looking for properties with motivated sellers that want to sell fast for cash, or houses that need some work. My partner has a construction team to fix up the houses after we buy them, but it's been difficult to find properties on the MLS that make sense. So I thought I'd see if you have any properties with motivated sellers coming up, or if you can ask around your office."

If no:
> "I'll send you my contact info... I'd love to have you write the offers for us, and this way you can keep both sides of the commission on all your listings."

(The "partner + construction team" ARE his cash buyers.)

> "you can keep both sides of the commission on all of your listings."

**FOLLOW-UP DRIP (GHL):** After a positive call — follow-up 1 within 7 days, follow-up 2 at ~14 days, then every 10–30 days. Mix transactional ("Any deals coming up?") with open-ended "disruptor" relationship texts (market opinion, contractor referrals). Always still ask "What can I buy right now?"

**DISCOVERY CALL (off-market):**
- Build rapport 15–30s.
- Ask for the address — if withheld, offer a property-specific Buyer Broker Agreement (BBA) to reassure them they get paid.
- MOST IMPORTANT question: "Can you catch me up to speed with the seller situation on this one?" / "What's the story on this house?"
- Assumption questions (owner-occupied / vacant? condition? roof/AC age?).
- "What makes the seller decide to sell?"
- Quick offer + ask for the counter, justify with ARV + rehab.

> "What's the story on this house?"

**COMMISSION FLEX:** Offer 3% buyer side OR the seller reduces price (unrepresented). Better — offer full 6% (up to 10%) "as if you were double dipping," and the agent tells the seller "you pay no commission, net to you."

> "How about I pay you the full 6% as if you were double dipping?"

**GEOGRAPHIC:** Target agents 45–60 min OUTSIDE the metro.

**TEAM LADDER:** Cold caller → Discovery → Closing.

## Numbers & benchmarks

- Since Nov 2023: **73 deals, $1.3M net, $17.3K avg fee** (industry $5–10K).
- ~7x return.
- Texts ~100 agents/day, ~30 reply.
- Ramp: 6–9 months.
- ~500 agents → 1 deal/mo; now running ~3,000 agents.
- Close ratio: 1 in 15 offers.
- Cold caller pay ~$20–25/hr.
- Tools: GoHighLevel, InvestorLift, InvestorBase.

> "For every 15 offers... we get one contract."
