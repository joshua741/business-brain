# Creative Financing

**Summary**: Umbrella term for non-traditional real estate financing structures including rent-to-own, subject-to, and seller finance.

**Sources**: CLAUDE.md seed

**Last updated**: 2026-05-28

---

The primary framework for both acquiring and exiting properties in the [[wih]] portfolio. Includes: [[rent-to-own]] (primary exit), [[subject-to]] (acquisition tool), and seller finance (both acquisition and exit). Avoids conventional bank financing where possible to preserve speed and flexibility. Reference: [[pace-morby]].
