# Morby Method

**Summary**: The seller-carryback structure (popularized by [[pace-morby]]) WIH uses to enter deals **net-zero** — the seller's equity / second-position note covers the buyer's down payment and closing costs, which are recouped at closing so no money comes out of pocket.

**Sources**: transcript 2026-06-18

**Last updated**: 2026-06-26

---

- Core rule (per Josh, Jun 18): 100% of the funds WIH brings to closing must be returned by the second position; if the seller's equity can't cover closing costs + down payment, the deal must be restructured or walked. Applied on the [[jacoby-maydwell]] [[4513-48th-st]] deal. (source: transcript-2026-06-18)
