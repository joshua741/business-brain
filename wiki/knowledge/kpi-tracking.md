# KPI Tracking

**Summary**: The metrics system behind [[wih]] acquisitions — daily agent activity trackers, funnel conversion benchmarks, ad-spend ROAS targets, and marketing-list campaign logs. Feeds off the [[ghl]] pipeline and [[google-sheets]].

**Sources**: team-goals-kpis.md; kpi-outreach.md; kpi-tracker-on-market-offers.md; kpi-offer-responder.md; kpi-low-equity-leads.md; kpi-astro-leads.md; ad-spend-kpis.md; marketing-campaigns.md; daily-intensity-calendar-2026.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

Joshua tracks KPIs daily — a core personal pattern (see [[joshua]]). The acquisition team's activity and conversion are measured across several [[google-sheets]] trackers tied to the [[ghl]] CRM and the [[sales-scripts]] / [[wholesale]] pipeline. Agents historically tracked: Octavia, Hafez, Kwin, [[mostafa]].

## Funnel conversion benchmarks (team-goals-kpis)
| Stage | Outbound | Inbound |
|---|---|---|
| Lead → Net | 80% | 65% |
| Net Lead → Appointment | 20% | 60% |
| Appointment → Offer | 85% | 85% |
| Offer → Contract | 15% | 20% |
| Contract → Close | 75% | 75% |
(source: team-goals-kpis.md)

## Daily activity trackers
- **KPI Outreach** — reached-out / contacted / prospecting / disqualified / leads (source: kpi-outreach.md).
- **KPI Tracker On-Market Offers** — card actions (goal 35+), new contacts (12–15), meetings (3+/day) (source: kpi-tracker-on-market-offers.md).
- **KPI Offer Responder** — total actions (goal 45+), sent-to-underwriter, offers, follow-ups; grades Working/Average/Momentum/Win (source: kpi-offer-responder.md).
- **KPI Low Equity Leads** — disposition-rate grades (Win 90%+, Momentum 85–89%) (source: kpi-low-equity-leads.md).
- **KPI Astro Leads** — older multi-market list-source tracker (PV/OF/R/AC) (source: kpi-astro-leads.md).

## Ad spend & marketing
- **Ad Spend KPIs** — CPL, ROAS, lead→deal %, cash-conversion-cycle; ROAS goals 3x (900k+ ARV), 5x (400–850k), 7x (<300k), CCC <90 days (source: ad-spend-kpis.md).
- **Marketing Campaigns** — list buckets (Equity, Stacked, Vacant, "Ouchies"/tax-delinquent/probate) and campaign log; current ops use GHL + BatchLeads + iSpeedToLead (source: marketing-campaigns.md).

## Personal intensity
- **Daily Intensity Calendar 2026** — weeks 1–48 (Feb 2026–Jan 2027), daily Fitness/Faith/Family/Finance blocks, Sunday weekly review; ties to the **$500 bet on 80% needle-mover completion by Aug 5, 2026** (source: daily-intensity-calendar-2026.md). See [[joshua]].
