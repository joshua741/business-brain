# MLS Two-Call Closing

**Summary**: [[alex-martinez]]'s (RealEstateSkills.com) on-market / MLS wholesale channel — a two-call discovery-then-close process that complements the off-market [[no-fluff-model]].

**Sources**: clip-2026-06-03-this-exact-wholesale-real-estate-script-made-1-2m-in-12-mont.md, clip-2026-06-03-start-wholesale-2026-FULL.md, clip-2026-06-03-agent-script-freescript-FULL.md

**Last updated**: 2026-06-03

[[alex-martinez]]'s on-market channel works the MLS instead of off-market lists — complementary to the off-market [[no-fluff-model]]. The process is two distinct calls.

## Call 1 — Discovery
A 15-part script. Gather information, make **NO** offer. Capture: seller motivation, condition, major issues, upgrades, ARV, number of offers received, price-to-lock + "how flexible," terms, review timing, and a deal-extraction question.

## Call 2 — Close
Re-establish rapport, state the all-cash offer + terms, then **be silent**.

## Key levers
- Get the listing agent to represent you = dual commission (~$12K vs. $6K, roughly 80% of the time).
- "MLS = Multiple **LEADS** Service" — ~90% of deals are on-market.
- Reverse-engineering KPI math: $10K fee = 1 deal/mo = 15 written offers/mo.
- "Objections are deal multipliers."
- 75% of houses sell below list.

The 15-part Discovery script doubles as a deal-intake data schema for [[wih-app]].
