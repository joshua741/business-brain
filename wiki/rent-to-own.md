# Rent-to-Own

**Summary**: Primary exit strategy for WIH properties. Tenant pays rent plus option consideration and builds toward purchase.

**Sources**: CLAUDE.md seed

**Last updated**: 2026-05-28

---

The dominant exit strategy in the [[wih]] portfolio. Tenant pays monthly rent plus an option fee that builds toward the eventual purchase price. Seller retains ownership until the tenant exercises their option. [[r2oc]] is the marketing brand for rent-to-own properties. Part of the broader [[creative-financing]] toolkit alongside [[subject-to]].
