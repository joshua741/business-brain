# PML / TL

**Summary**: Private Money Lenders and Transactional Lenders. The capital raising vertical of WIH automated via Vince AI.

**Sources**: CLAUDE.md seed

**Last updated**: 2026-05-28

---

Two types of private capital sources for [[wih]] deals. Private Money Lenders (PML) provide longer-term capital for acquisitions and holds. Transactional Lenders (TL) provide short-term bridge capital for wholesale assignments and quick flips. Top-of-funnel qualification is automated via [[vince-ai]] running on [[ghl]] + [[twilio]].
