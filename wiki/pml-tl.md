# PML / TL

**Summary**: Private Money Lenders and Transactional Lenders. The capital raising vertical of WIH automated via Vince AI.

**Sources**: CLAUDE.md seed; vince-master-booklet.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

Two types of private capital sources for [[wih]] deals. Top-of-funnel qualification is automated via [[vince-ai]].

## Program terms (per Vince booklet)
- **PML — Private Money Lenders:** capital secured by a recorded Deed of Trust + promissory note; **10–12% annual** over **2–5 year** notes; **$10,000 minimum** liquid; serviced through Evergreen Note Servicing; no cross-collateral, first position typically in place (source: vince-master-booklet.md). See [[loan-servicing]].
- **TL — Transactional Lenders:** short-term bridge capital for double closes; **flat 10% per transaction**, funds in/out in **24–48 hours**; **$5,000 minimum**; no long-term commitment (source: vince-master-booklet.md).

Documentation uses the promissory note + deed of trust template (see [[legal-document-templates]]). Whole-life policy cash value ([[infinite-banking]]) is a complementary internal capital source.
