# Seller Finance

**Summary**: Owner-financing exit/acquisition structure where the buyer takes the deed at closing and pays the seller (WIH) directly on a 30-year note instead of a bank. Marketed through [[r2oc]] alongside [[rent-to-own]].

**Sources**: rent-to-own-and-seller-finance-faq.md; faq-rent-to-own.md; seller-financing-description.md; credit-repair-email-seller-finance.md; down-payment-calculator-seller-finance-and-rto.md; seller-finance-agreement-526-53r-st.md

**Last updated**: 2026-05-29

---

A core [[creative-financing]] tool. Buyer becomes the legal owner at closing and pays [[wih]] directly on a note structured like a mortgage (principal, interest, taxes, insurance). No bank involved. Title is held in a trust with the buyer as beneficiary to secure the arrangement (source: seller-financing-description.md).

## Program terms (R2OC)
- 30-year fully amortized note; title transfers at closing (source: rent-to-own-and-seller-finance-faq.md).
- Min credit **600+** (560–599 with 3.5x income); income **3x** monthly payment (source: rent-to-own-and-seller-finance-faq.md).
- **5% prepayment penalty** if refinanced/sold within first 5 years (waived after 60 months); due-on-sale clause prevents wrapping/transferring while active; third-party serviced (source: rent-to-own-and-seller-finance-faq.md). See [[loan-servicing]].
- Down-payment / interest-rate scale used in credit-repair outreach: 10% down = 8% rate, sliding to 5% down = 13% rate (source: credit-repair-email-seller-finance.md).

## Qualifying from RTO into seller finance
A [[rent-to-own]] tenant can convert to seller finance based on on-time payment history (80%+); deposit waived/reduced based on lateness over months 13–36 (source: rent-to-own-and-seller-finance-faq.md).

## Lead generation
- **Credit-repair referral channel:** outreach to credit-repair specialists offering the buyer's first month's payment as a referral fee per closed client; intake via myspeedycashoffer.com/sellerfinanceform (source: credit-repair-email-seller-finance.md). Routes through [[mostafa]] / [[ghl]].
- Down-payment calculator used to structure deals (e.g. $140k price → $1,400/mo) (source: down-payment-calculator-seller-finance-and-rto.md).

## Example deals
- **126 Paul St, San Antonio TX:** $285,000 price, 10% down, 7% rate, 30-yr, ~$2,175.62/mo (source: seller-financing-description.md).
- **526 53rd St, Lubbock:** wrap-around contract-for-deed at $85,000, $12,000 down, 10%, $911/mo, over a Happy State Bank underlying loan (source: seller-finance-agreement-526-53r-st.md). See [[source-seller-finance-526-53rd]].
