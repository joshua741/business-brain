1. Purpose of Move-In Deposit

This deposit is paid solely to reserve the property for Rent-to-Own or Seller Financing consideration. It is not a security deposit, rent payment, mortgage payment, down payment, or guarantee of possession.

2. Non-Refundable Nature

The deposit is non-refundable unless the property becomes unavailable for reasons not caused by the applicant/buyer. The deposit may be forfeited if any of the following occur:

  • Failure to make required rent/mortgage, remaining deposit, or any related cost within 2 days of the scheduled due date (or 7-day advance written/verbal notice)
  • Failure to demonstrate sufficient income to meet the required payment
  • Decision or inability to proceed for any reason
  • Refusal to sign required documents
  • Submission of fraudulent information on the application
  • Refusal to provide ACH information for automatic drafts
  • (Seller Finance only) Inability to provide proof of home insurance within 7 days of closing

3. Payment Deadline

If move-in occurs on the 25th, the full rent/mortgage amount is due on that date. If move-in occurs before or after the 25th, a prorated payment is required.

4. Changes to Due Dates

Extensions (pushing dates backward) require a written amendment signed by both parties. Acceleration (moving dates forward) may be issued at seller/lender discretion without requiring the applicant's signature.

5. Property Availability

If the applicant/buyer fails to submit the first payment or remaining deposit within 2 days of the due date, the property may be re-listed and the holding fee may be forfeited.
